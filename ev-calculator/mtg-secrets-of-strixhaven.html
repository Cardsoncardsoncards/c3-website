Is Secrets of Strixhaven Worth Opening in Australia?

Secrets of Strixhaven is a 65-card supplemental set reprinting the most iconic Mystical Archive cards from the original Strixhaven: School of Mages, plus exclusive new cards. It is not a Standard-legal set, which means its value is driven entirely by eternal format playability and collector demand.

For Australian players, the AU$259.95 Amazon AU price point (at time of publication) puts the box at borderline EV at release. The key cards driving value are the foil Mystical Archive reprints, which include powerful spells like Counterspell, Swords to Plowshares, and Time Warp. These have strong Commander and Legacy demand but are also freely available as singles.

Why singles may be the better play

If you need specific Mystical Archive cards for your decks, buying singles is almost always more efficient than cracking boxes. The EV only favours box opening if you plan to sell the bulk commons and uncommons, which requires effort and time. The collector case for opening

If you are a collector who enjoys the opening experience, or you are pulling for foil versions of specific Mystical Archive cards, the box has genuine appeal. The etched foil and special treatment versions can significantly exceed the average card value estimates above if you pull well.

How does expected value work for MTG booster boxes?
EV is calculated by multiplying the pull rate for each rarity by the average market value of cards at that rarity level, then summing across all slots in a box. It gives you the average dollar return across many openings. Individual boxes will vary significantly from this average.
Should I buy singles instead of opening Secrets of Strixhaven?
For most players, yes. If you have specific Mystical Archive cards you want, buying singles directly from our eBay store is more cost-efficient than cracking boxes and hoping to hit them. Singles are available at market price with no variance risk.
Is Strixhaven Standard legal?
Secrets of Strixhaven is not Standard legal. It is a supplemental set. The cards inside are legal in the same formats as their original printings (Legacy, Commander, Vintage as applicable). Check individual card legality on Scryfall.
