How We Calculate EV for Australian Players

Expected value (EV) is the average dollar return from opening a pack. We calculate it by multiplying the official pull rate for each rarity by the current AUD market value of cards at that rarity level, then summing across all pack slots. All pricing is in AUD, not USD conversions from US-focused sites.

Pull rates come from Wizards of the Coast's official published data. Card values are sourced from recent eBay AU sold listings and MTGGoldfish data converted at the live exchange rate. Play Booster and Collector Booster EV are calculated separately as they have completely different pack contents, pull rates, and price points. Toggle between both views above.

Each set page shows a data confidence rating. High means official pull rates and active AU sales history. Low means the AU market is thin or pull rates are community-sourced. EV is directional, not a guarantee. It helps you decide whether to open sealed product or buy the specific singles you need directly.
