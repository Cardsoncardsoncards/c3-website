Common Questions

Will these tools be free?
Most tools will be free with no account required, following the same model as our TCG card vault and collection tracker. Some advanced features may have a one-time premium upgrade, similar to our existing Etsy spreadsheet tools. We will always have a fully functional free tier.
When will they be available?
We are gauging demand before committing to a build timeline. Register your interest above and you will be first to know when the first tool goes live.
What edition is being supported?
Initially 5th Edition (5e) and D&D 2024 (the revised ruleset). We will expand based on what the community wants.
Do you already sell D&D products?
Yes. Our Etsy shop has printable D&D resources including dungeon puzzles, trap cards, tavern scene decks, and DM tools. All instant download, AUD pricing.
