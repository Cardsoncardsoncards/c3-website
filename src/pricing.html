Common questions

Is the free plan really free?

Yes. Card prices, the tools, Card Compare and the quizzes cost nothing and need no card. The paid tiers add depth for people who buy and sell often.

Why pay when other price sites are free?

Free tools mostly show converted USD prices. C3 is built around Australian pricing and AU seller signals across 32 games, including ones nobody else tracks locally. You are paying for AU accuracy and time saved, not raw lookups.

What is in the Seller report?

A weekly email covering the biggest AU price movers across all 31 tracked games, the top buy and sell signals, and where to focus your listing effort that week. Delivered to your inbox every Monday. Nothing to log into.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel any time via the subscriber portal or by emailing ccc.squadhelp@gmail.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, so you keep access until then.
