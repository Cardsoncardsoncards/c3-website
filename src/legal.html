7. Sealed Product & Investment Risk Disclaimer

Important: Sealed trading card products (booster boxes, elite trainer boxes, collector boosters, and similar items) are not regulated financial instruments. Buying sealed product as an investment carries significant risk of capital loss.

Cards on Cards on Cards may display pricing data, expected value estimates, price history, and market information for sealed trading card products including but not limited to booster boxes, elite trainer boxes, collector boosters, starter decks, and bundle products.

Sealed product pricing

Sealed product prices shown on this site are estimates based on eBay AU sold listings and other third-party data sources. Prices are indicative only. Actual resale prices may vary significantly based on condition, timing, platform fees, shipping costs, and market demand at the time of sale.

Expected value and ROI estimates

Where expected value (EV), return on investment (ROI), or similar estimates are displayed for sealed products, these are mathematical calculations based on current single card prices and published pull rates. They represent a statistical average across many openings and do not predict what any individual will pull from a specific box. Individual results will vary substantially. Most boxes opened will yield cards with a total market value below the purchase price of the box.

Sealed product as investment

Some collectors purchase sealed trading card products with the intention of reselling them at a higher price in the future. Cards on Cards on Cards does not endorse, encourage, or advise this as an investment strategy. Past price appreciation of sealed products does not guarantee future appreciation. Sealed product values can and do decline. Factors including reprints, set rotations, game discontinuation, changes in player interest, and general market conditions can cause significant and rapid declines in sealed product value.

Cards on Cards on Cards is not a financial adviser. Nothing on this site constitutes financial, investment, or professional advice. If you are considering purchasing sealed products as a significant financial investment, we strongly recommend seeking independent financial advice from a licensed Australian financial adviser.

No guarantee of liquidity

The market for trading card singles and sealed products is not a regulated exchange. There is no guaranteed buyer for any product at any price. Liquidity may be limited, particularly for less popular games or older product.
