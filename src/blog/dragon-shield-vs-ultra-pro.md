## Dragon Shield vs Ultra Pro: Which Sleeves Are Worth It in Australia?

Card sleeves are the most-purchased TCG accessory in Australia. Every player needs them. The two dominant brands are Dragon Shield and Ultra Pro, and the choice between them comes down to what game you play, how much shuffling your deck does, and whether you care about opaque backs.

This guide covers both brands head-to-head with AU pricing from Amazon.

## Dragon Shield: The Premium Pick

Dragon Shield Mattes are the industry benchmark for durability. The laminate holds up through thousands of shuffles. The downside is price: roughly AU$18 to $22 per pack of 100 sleeves, which is 18 to 22 cents per sleeve.

**Best for:** MTG (Commander or Competitive), Pokemon, Lorcana, One Piece.

## Which One to Buy

If you are playing a competitive format or care about longevity: Dragon Shield Mattes.

If you need to sleeve a large casual collection on a budget: Ultra Pro Eclipse.

The price gap per deck is about AU$4 to $6. On one deck that is negligible. On 10 decks it starts to matter.
