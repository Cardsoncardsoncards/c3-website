## What Is a Pre-Rift Event?

Pre-Rift Events are the Riftbound equivalent of a prerelease. They run at authorised Riftbound retail locations in the days before a new set reaches general retail. You pay the entry fee, receive a sealed Vendetta product allocation, and build a deck from what you open to play in the event.

The format is sealed, which means no existing card collection is required. Everyone is working with the same variable resource: the packs they opened.

The AU$65 entry fee covers both your sealed product and your event entry. You keep everything you pull.

Pre-Rift Events are one of the most practical ways to experience a new Riftbound set because the sealed environment gives every player immediate access to the new cards in a competitive context.

## How Sealed Works in Riftbound

Open your full Vendetta sealed allocation and sort all cards by champion affiliation and card type. Choose one champion as your primary champion. Build your combat deck around that champion's card pool.

Riftbound sealed decks are built from what you opened, not from the optimal constructed card list. Prioritise cards that interact directly with your chosen champion's abilities. Champion synergy matters more in sealed than in constructed, where access to the full card pool gives you optimised options.

Set aside any cards with significant standalone value before building. Vendetta singles that are worth AU$20 or more individually represent real monetary value from your entry fee. Know what you opened before deciding whether to play those cards or protect them separately.
