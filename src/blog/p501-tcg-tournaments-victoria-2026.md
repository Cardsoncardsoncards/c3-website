## Yu-Gi-Oh Victoria 2026

**10 to 12 July Yu-Gi-Oh Oceanic World Championship Qualifier**
Malvern Town Hall, 1251 High Street, Malvern VIC 3144. The most important Yu-Gi-Oh event in Australia this year. Top finishers earn World Championship invitations. Advanced Constructed. Organised by Konami.
Register: [events.humanitix.com/yu-gi-oh-oceanic-championships-2026](https://events.humanitix.com/yu-gi-oh-oceanic-championships-2026)
Card prices: [C3 Yu-Gi-Oh hub](/cards/yugioh)

The Oceanic WCQ at Malvern Town Hall is accessible by tram from Melbourne CBD via routes 5 and 6 on High Street. For competitive YGO players in Victoria, this is the event of the year.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the biggest TCG event in Victoria in 2026?**
For competitive play, the Yu-Gi-Oh Oceanic WCQ at Malvern Town Hall on 10-12 July is the highest-stakes event with World Championship qualification on the line. For community play, CommandFest Melbourne on 4-6 December at the MCEC is the largest Magic event of the year in Victoria.

**Where are TCG events held in Melbourne?**
Major events use the MCEC (South Wharf), Marvel Stadium (Docklands), and dedicated local game stores including Plenty of Games (CBD Collins Street), Vault Games (various), and Good Games (various Melbourne locations). La Trobe University in Bundoora hosts Bandai TAK Games Regionals.

**How do I find weekly TCG events near me in Victoria?**
Check the event pages for your specific game's organiser. Good Games stores post their weekly schedules at goodgames.com.au. Vault Games events are at vaultgames.com.au. For Riftbound, use locator.riftbound.uvsgames.com. For Magic, locator.wizards.com shows weekly events by postcode.

**Are all Victorian events open to players of all skill levels?**
Prereleases and weekly locals events are explicitly designed for all skill levels. Regional Championships and the WCQ are open to all players but are competitive-level events. Start with weekly locals before entering a Regional if you are new to organised play.

**Where can I buy competitive cards in VIC before a tournament?**
Check [C3](/cards) for current AUD prices first. Local Victorian stores including Good Games, Vault Games, and independent specialists carry competitive singles. eBay AU is the most liquid secondary market for most games.
