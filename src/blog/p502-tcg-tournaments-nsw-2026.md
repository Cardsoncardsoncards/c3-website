## Preparing for NSW Events

Card prices rise in the weeks before major events as NSW competitive players finalise their decks. The Hurstville and Bankstown precincts see particularly concentrated demand before MTG Super Series and Bandai Regional events given the cluster of large venues in those suburbs.

> **Check prices before buying for any NSW tournament**
>
> The [C3 Card Vault](/cards) tracks AUD prices across all games listed on this page, updated daily. Check what your target cards cost before going to a store or eBay. Buying 3 to 4 weeks before a major event consistently saves money versus buying in the final week.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the biggest TCG event in NSW in 2026?**
Bushiroad Summer Fest at Bankstown Sports Club on 5-6 September is the largest multi-game event in NSW for 2026, covering Vanguard, Shadowverse Evolve, and Weiss Schwarz. The MTG ANZ Super Series Regional #13 at Club Central Hurstville on 23-25 October is the largest single-game event.

**Where are most major TCG events held in Sydney?**
The Hurstville and Bankstown areas in south-western Sydney host Bandai TCG events, MTG Super Series events, and Konami events. Good Games Central on George Street in the CBD is the main hub for weekly events. For Riftbound events, check Sydney Olympic Park area stores.

**Where can I find weekly TCG events near me in NSW?**
Good Games stores across NSW post their weekly event schedules at goodgames.com.au. For Pokemon, use play.limitlesstcg.com. For Magic, locator.wizards.com. For Riftbound, locator.riftbound.uvsgames.com. For Yu-Gi-Oh, yugioh-card.com/en/events.

**Where can I buy competitive TCG cards in NSW?**
Check [C3](/cards) for current AUD prices first. Good Games stores across NSW stock competitive singles for most games. eBay AU is the most liquid secondary market. Local game stores in Sydney CBD and Parramatta areas also carry competitive stock for major games.
