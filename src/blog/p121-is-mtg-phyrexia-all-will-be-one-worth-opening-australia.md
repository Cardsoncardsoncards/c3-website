## What Is Phyrexia: All Will Be One and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Phyrexia: All Will Be One is a standard-legal Play Booster set with 36 packs per box. The Phyrexian text treatment appears in both Play Boosters (as borderless Phyrexian text versions) and Collector Boosters (as Oil Slick Raised Foil versions — the premium exclusive treatment). The Poison counter mechanic (dealing 10 Poison counters to a player wins the game) and Proliferate (adding counters to permanents and players) drive competitive combo strategies. The full Praetor cycle appears with Elesh Norn as the headliner.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the Phyrexian text treatment?**
The Phyrexian text treatment prints card text in the Phyrexian language — a constructed fictional alphabet developed for the Phyrexian faction in MTG lore. The effect creates a visually striking card where all rules text is unreadable without a translation reference. These cards are functionally identical to regular versions but command collector premiums due to their unique aesthetic. Elesh Norn in Phyrexian text is the most sought after.

**Is ONE worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$170, Phyrexia: All Will Be One offers solid EV above the typical standard set average. The Phyrexian text treatment ceiling is high — Elesh Norn in Phyrexian text is worth AU$20-50+ and pulling one significantly boosts box value. Competitive staples in Poison and Proliferate archetypes maintain ongoing demand. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current assessment.

**What is the Oil Slick Raised Foil treatment?**
Oil Slick Raised Foil is a Collector Booster exclusive treatment in Phyrexia: All Will Be One. It applies an iridescent oil-slick colour effect to the foiling with raised physical texture on the card surface. It is the premium version above the standard Phyrexian text treatment and is exclusive to Collector Boosters. Oil Slick Raised Foil versions of Praetors command the highest premiums in the AU secondary market.

**What happened with Sheoldred the Apocalypse from this set?**
Sheoldred the Apocalypse (from Dominaria United, the previous set) is the most valuable Phyrexian character card of the invasion arc period. The ONE Sheoldred is Sheoldred Whispering One in a Phyrexian text treatment rather than the standard version. The Dominaria United Sheoldred was worth AU$40-80+ at peak. The Phyrexian text Sheoldred in ONE commands collector premiums.
