MTG Jumpstart released July 2020 with a design philosophy unlike any other MTG booster product: themed packs meant to be shuffled together to create instant playable decks. Each Jumpstart pack has a theme — Pirates, Dinosaurs, Unicorns, Phyrexians, and dozens more — and shuffling any two packs together creates a coherent 40-card deck ready to play.

This design created a product category that sits between a precon Commander deck and a standard booster box. For experienced players the interest is less the format and more the Jumpstart-exclusive cards — cards that cannot be found in any other booster product.

## What Is Jumpstart and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Jumpstart contains 46 different themed packs each with a distinct 20-card configuration. Every booster box contains 24 packs in random combinations. The themed packs include reprints alongside new Jumpstart-exclusive cards. Key exclusive cards include Allosaurus Shepherd (Legacy and Commander staple), Tinybones Trinket Thief (Commander staple), Inquisitive Puppet (Commander enabler), and Phyrexian Tower (Legacy and Commander land reprint). The 20-card pack structure makes EV calculation different from standard booster products.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does Jumpstart EV calculation work differently from standard sets?**
Jumpstart's themed pack structure means EV depends heavily on which of the 46 themed packs appear in your box. Each theme has a fixed card list — some themes contain high-value exclusives, others are entirely low-value reprints. The EV Calculator provides an average across themes, but individual box results vary significantly based on theme combination. The exclusive cards (Allosaurus Shepherd, Tinybones) appear in specific themes only.

**Is Jumpstart worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$155, Jumpstart offers moderate EV with high theme-based variance. If your box contains the Elf or Phyrexian themes (which include the most valuable exclusives) EV is strong. Other theme combinations produce lower values. Use the C3 EV Calculator as an average guide rather than a precise prediction.

**What makes Allosaurus Shepherd so valuable?**
Allosaurus Shepherd is a one-mana green legendary 1/1 that cannot be countered by spells or abilities. While it is on the battlefield, your green spells cannot be countered either. In Legacy it enables Elves decks to play through Force of Will. In Commander it protects key green spells from counterspells in blue-heavy metas. The combination of uncounterability and the ability to grant it to all your green spells makes it a premium Legacy and Commander tool.

**Will Jumpstart exclusives ever be reprinted elsewhere?**
Some Jumpstart exclusives have been reprinted in subsequent products — Jumpstart 2022 and various Commander precons have included some. Allosaurus Shepherd was reprinted in Commander Masters (2023) which reduced its price from AU$40-60+ down to AU$20-40+. Other exclusives remain unique to Jumpstart. Check current eBay AU pricing for the most accurate assessment of whether buying the original Jumpstart box or a reprint is more cost-efficient for specific cards.
