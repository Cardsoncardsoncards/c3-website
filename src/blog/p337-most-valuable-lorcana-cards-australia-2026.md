## Quick Answer

The most valuable Disney Lorcana cards in Australia in 2026 are Enchanted Rare versions of iconic Disney characters, with values ranging from AU$80 to AU$600 or more for the most sought copies. The Enchanted tier is Lorcana's equivalent of Pokemon's Special Illustration Rares: full-art, unique illustrations of beloved characters. ## The Enchanted Tier: Where Lorcana Value Lives

Ravensburger designed the Lorcana rarity system with a distinct premium tier called Enchanted Rares. These are alternate-art versions of existing Rare and Legendary cards with completely redrawn artwork in a unique visual style, typically featuring gold foil treatments and cinematic compositions.

Enchanted Rares are the rarest standard-packable cards in Lorcana. Pull rates are approximately one per two to four booster boxes depending on the set, with the specific card within the Enchanted pool being random.

**Elsa, Spirit of Winter (Enchanted)** from The First Chapter has consistently been among the most valuable Enchanted rares since the game launched. Elsa's character popularity drives collector demand beyond what the card's game statistics would suggest.

**Mickey Mouse variants** as Enchanted cards from various sets hold strong value because of Mickey's position as Disney's most iconic character. Any Mickey Enchanted from The First Chapter or Rise of the Floodborn commands attention.

**Moana, of Motunui (Enchanted)** and other Moana cards have maintained value as her character resonates strongly with the Lorcana collector base.

The value of any specific Enchanted card depends heavily on the character depicted. Enchanted versions of fan-favourite Disney characters (Elsa, Moana, Simba, Maleficent, Ursula) trade at meaningful premiums over Enchanted versions of less-popular characters from the same set.

## Legendary Cards

Legendary is the rarest non-Enchanted rarity in Lorcana. These full-art cards feature characters at iconic moments and appear approximately once per two packs. The most competitively relevant and visually striking Legendary cards from each set command values in the AU$30 to AU$80 range.

Legendary cards with both competitive value in current deck formats and strong character appeal sit at the top of this range. As Lorcana evolves and the competitive scene grows in Australia, the ceiling on high-impact Legendaries may rise.

## Set-by-Set Highlights

**The First Chapter (Set 1)** remains the reference point for Lorcana value. Early set cards are not reprinted in the same way because sets rotate out of print eventually. Enchanted Rares from Set 1 carry a scarcity premium that later sets do not yet have.

**Rise of the Floodborn (Set 2)** introduced new characters and a new visual treatment approach that resonated with collectors. Its Enchanted pool includes several high-demand characters.

**Into the Inklands (Set 3)** and subsequent sets have expanded the character roster and introduced new gameplay mechanics. Current set Enchanted Rares are most relevant for active players and recent collectors.

**The Wilds of the Unknown (current)** is the active set as of mid-2026. Its Enchanted pool is still being discovered by the Australian market. Check [C3 Lorcana card prices](/cards/lorcana) for current pricing as the market establishes itself.

## Cold Foil and Special Treatments

Some Lorcana cards appear with cold foil treatments at specific card shops and events. These are not standard booster pull rates and represent a distinct collector tier with very limited supply. If you encounter cold foil Lorcana cards, their value is significantly higher than standard Enchanted versions of the same card.

## The Lorcana Competitive Angle

Lorcana is growing its competitive scene in Australia. As more organised play events run, competitively viable cards gain an additional demand driver on top of their collector appeal. Cards that are both Enchanted and competitively strong represent the highest ceiling in the market.

## The C3 Take

Lorcana's value proposition for Australian collectors is clear: it is a premium product with genuine Disney IP at its core, and Enchanted Rares of beloved characters have proven to hold their value well since launch. The collector base for Lorcana overlaps significantly with Disney fans who are not traditional TCG players, which creates demand dynamics that differ from games like MTG or Pokemon. The result is that character appeal often matters more than competitive relevance when predicting which Enchanted cards will hold the most value. If you are buying Lorcana as a collector, focus on Enchanted versions of Disney's most iconic characters. If you are playing competitively, check the current meta and buy singles rather than opening product.
