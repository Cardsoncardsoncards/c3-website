**What tribes are in Lorwyn Eclipsed?**
The five primary tribes are Faeries (Blue-Black), Treefolk (Green-White), Giants (Red-White), Merrows (Blue-Green), and Kithkin (White). Each tribe has legendary creatures, tribal payoffs, and synergistic cards across multiple rarities. Faeries and Treefolk have the longest histories in MTG and typically command the strongest Commander demand.

**Why is the original Lorwyn block significant in MTG history?**
The original Lorwyn block (2007-2008) is significant for two major reasons: it introduced Planeswalker cards to the game for the first time (now one of the most iconic card types), and it established a tribal-focused set design template that influenced many subsequent MTG sets. Lorwyn's five tribes had distinctive mechanical identities and the plane's cozy autumnal aesthetic was a beloved departure from the darker themes of other planes.

**How does Lorwyn Eclipsed compare to Kaldheim for tribal Commander EV?**
Kaldheim (Norse mythology, 2021) has strong God tribal and Dwarf, Elf, and Viking tribal support. Lorwyn Eclipsed has Faerie, Treefolk, Giant, Merrow, and Kithkin tribal support. Faerie tribal is more developed in Commander than most Kaldheim tribes, giving Lorwyn Eclipsed a slight edge for dedicated tribal Commander players. Both sets sit in similar borderline EV territory at their respective price points.
