## What Is Riftbound?

Riftbound is Riot Games' official physical trading card game based on the League of Legends universe. It launched in 2025 and has been building a player base in Australia, particularly among existing League of Legends players who are making the transition from the digital game to a physical card game.

The game uses a champion-based format where each player builds a deck centred around a League of Legends champion card. Champions have unique abilities that reflect their in-game counterparts, which gives the game a strong appeal for existing League players. You will recognise Jinx, Vi, Caitlyn, Jayce, and other champions from the Runeterra universe in the card designs.

Riftbound has several structural advantages over many newer TCG entrants. Riot Games has a massive existing player base through League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics. The Arcane Netflix series has expanded the Runeterra IP's reach well beyond the gaming audience. And Riot has significant resources to support organised play infrastructure in Australia.

## Is Riftbound Worth Getting Into?

Honest assessment for Australian players:

**The case for getting in now:** Riftbound is still in its early growth phase in Australia. Getting in early on a TCG with strong IP backing and a well-resourced publisher means you are building a collection while card prices are relatively accessible, before the game potentially becomes significantly more popular. The League of Legends player base in Australia is substantial, and as more of them become aware of the card game, demand for competitive singles tends to increase.

There is also the standard risk with any newer TCG that the organised play ecosystem takes time to develop to the level of Pokemon or MTG in Australia.

**The bottom line:** If you are a League of Legends player who has been curious about Riftbound, Vendetta is a reasonable entry point. A starter set or a single booster box to try the game is a low-risk buy. If you are a TCG-first player evaluating whether to add Riftbound to your rotation, watch the community response to Vendetta in August before committing to a booster box.
