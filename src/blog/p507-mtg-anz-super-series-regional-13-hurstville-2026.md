## What Is the ANZ MTG Super Series?

The ANZ MTG Super Series is Australia and New Zealand's premier Magic: The Gathering Regional Championship circuit, run independently with affiliation to the official Wizards of the Coast organised play structure. The circuit runs multiple Regional Championship events per year across Australia and New Zealand, with each event awarding Regional Championship points and invitations to higher-level play including Pro Tour qualification pathways.

Regional Championships in the ANZ circuit are significant competitive events drawing players from across NSW, QLD, VIC, and beyond. Hurstville has been a recurring venue for the series due to Club Central's capacity and the location's transport accessibility from Sydney CBD and surrounding areas.

## Venue: Club Central Hurstville

Club Central Hurstville is at 2 Crofts Avenue, Hurstville, in Sydney's south. It is accessible by train from Central Station via the Illawarra Line, approximately 30 minutes. The Hurstville area has been a consistent Magic event hub in Sydney, also hosting Bandai game events including the Dragon Ball Super Sydney Regional in August.

The venue has ample space for large-format three-day Magic events, including main event rounds, top cut play, and side events running simultaneously. Food and beverage are available on-site at Club Central.

If you are travelling to Hurstville from outside Sydney, the location is close to multiple hotel options in the Hurstville and St George area. Sydney Airport is approximately 20 minutes by train via the Illawarra Line with one connection.
