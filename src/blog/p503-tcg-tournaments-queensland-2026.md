## Venue Guide: Brisbane Convention and Exhibition Centre

The BCEC is the primary large-event venue in Queensland for TCG competitions. It is located in South Brisbane at Grey Street, accessible from South Bank Station (2-minute walk) and Roma Street Station (via Goodwill Bridge). Multiple hotels are within walking distance in the South Bank precinct.

For players travelling from regional Queensland or interstate, the BCEC's South Brisbane position is convenient: Brisbane Airport connects to the city via Airtrain to Roma Street, which is a short walk or train ride to South Bank.

The cluster of Bandai Regional events on 18 and 19 July at the BCEC means players attending multiple game Regionals can stay in the same South Brisbane hotel for two or three nights and attend events across four separate games without changing venues.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the biggest TCG event in Queensland in 2026?**
For competitive play, Magic Spotlight: The Hobbit at the BCEC on 28-30 August is the highest-profile event. For the most events in a single weekend, the BCEC double-header on 18-19 July covers Gundam, Dragon Ball Super MASTERS, Dragon Ball Super Fusion World, and Digimon all in the same building across two consecutive days.

**Where are major TCG events held in Brisbane?**
The Brisbane Convention and Exhibition Centre in South Brisbane is the primary venue for Bandai game Regionals and Magic Spotlights. Vault Games Brisbane City on Queen Street hosts regular Lorcana events. Good Games Brisbane in Spring Hill hosts Riftbound events including the Vendetta Pre-Rift Event.

**How do I find weekly TCG events in Brisbane?**
Good Games Brisbane and Vault Games Brisbane City post their weekly schedules online. For Pokemon, use play.limitlesstcg.com. For Riftbound, use locator.riftbound.uvsgames.com. For Magic, locator.wizards.com. For Bandai games, check TAK Games social media and the individual game's official pages.

**Where can I buy competitive cards in QLD before a tournament?**
Check [C3](/cards) for current AUD prices first. Good Games Brisbane and Vault Games Brisbane City stock competitive singles for most games. eBay AU is the most liquid secondary market for harder-to-find singles.
