## What Is in Into the Inklands?

Into the Inklands introduced Location cards featuring Disney settings like The Pride Lands, Agrabah, and other iconic film environments. Characters at Locations gain abilities or bonus lore, creating positional strategy the game lacked before. The 204-card base set plus Enchanted variants introduced this mechanic while covering adventure-focused Disney properties.
