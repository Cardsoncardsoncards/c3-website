Mono-red Commander is polarising: it plays faster than any other colour, loses steam faster too, but has produced several commanders that consistently win at high-power tables. The key is choosing a commander that generates card advantage, since red's natural weakness is running out of cards.

## What Mono-Red Does Best

Haste. Speed. Direct damage. Mono-red can empty its hand faster than any colour and rebuild through wheels (spells that make everyone discard and redraw). Red also has access to strong artifact synergies and the best chaos effects in the format.

## Top Mono-Red Commanders

### Actual Top Choices

**Krenko, Mob Boss**: generates Goblin tokens equal to the number of Goblins you control every time he taps. With haste and tap effects, Krenko creates exponential token numbers.

**Current AU price**: approximately AU$5 to AU$12. Very accessible.

**Magda, Brazen Outlaw**: every Dwarf you tap for any reason adds a Treasure. Spend 5 Treasures to search for an artifact or Dragon and put it into play directly from your deck. Enables explosive artifact tutoring.

**Current AU price**: approximately AU$4 to AU$10.

**Gut, True Soul Zealot**: Skeleton tribal that converts Skeletons into threats and generates consistent value through sacrifice.

**Current AU price**: approximately AU$2 to AU$5. Budget-friendly.

**Torbran, Thane of Red Fell**: each source you control that deals damage to an opponent deals 2 additional damage. Makes every small damage source lethal. Combines with Purphoros, God of the Forge (enters the battlefield triggers) for consistent damage output.

**Current AU price**: approximately AU$5 to AU$12.

## Essential Mono-Red Commander Cards

- **Purphoros, God of the Forge** (AU$15 to AU$30): the best mono-red card advantage engine
- **Cursed Mirror** (AU$3 to AU$8): flexible utility artifact
- **Wheel of Fortune** (AU$80 to AU$120): premium refill — only include if budget allows
- **Reforge the Soul** (AU$3 to AU$8): budget wheel alternative

## Budget Options Under AU$10

Krenko, Mob Boss, Magda, Brazen Outlaw, and Torbran are all under AU$12 and represent the strongest accessible mono-red choices.
