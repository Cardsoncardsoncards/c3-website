## What Is Wilds Unknown?

Wilds Unknown is the twelfth Disney Lorcana set. It introduces Pixar characters to the game for the first time, including Toy Story, The Incredibles, and Brave. Woody, Buzz Lightyear, Mrs. Incredible, Jack-Jack, and Merida are among the confirmed characters. It also introduces the Traveler card mechanic, a new Two-Player Starter Set format, and prerelease packs for the first time in the game's history.

The addition of Pixar expands the collector base significantly. Players who grew up with these films and have never engaged with Lorcana before now have a direct reason to buy in. Ravensburger has confirmed Pixar's presence continues into future sets with Monsters, Inc. and Turning Red in Q3 2026 and Coco in Q4.

## Every Confirmed Product

### Booster Display (24 Packs)

The main sealed product. 24 booster packs per display, each pack containing 12 randomised cards. Standard Lorcana rarity breakdown applies: 6 commons, 3 uncommons, 2 rares or higher, and 1 foil of random rarity per pack.

Expected retail pricing is broadly comparable to previous sets, around AU$100 to AU$130 for a display, depending on retailer. Amazon AU has not yet listed the Wilds Unknown display as of mid-April. Once an ASIN is confirmed, the C3 shop page will be updated immediately.

This is the right product for collectors who want significant volume, players building competitive decks across the new card pool, and those chasing the set's Special Rarity chase cards.

### Two-Player Starter Set (Elastigirl and Buzz Lightyear)

New for Wilds Unknown and replacing the previous single-player starter deck format, the Two-Player Starter Set includes two complete 60-card decks, foil promo cards, playmats, lore trackers, and tokens. The two decks are Amethyst/Ruby (Elastigirl) and Amber/Emerald (Buzz Lightyear).

Expected retail around AU$55 to AU$70. This is the best product for anyone buying Lorcana for the first time, for gift purchases, and for existing players who want a friend to try the game without the barrier of a separate purchase.

The format change from single starter decks to a two-player set is a meaningful improvement. You can start playing immediately without needing to buy two separate products.

### Illumineer's Trove

Lorcana's bundle format. Approximately AU$50 to AU$65 based on comparable pricing from previous sets. Includes 8 booster packs plus storage accessories, deck box, and damage counters. A strong mid-tier option for players who want product variety without committing to a full display.

### Prerelease Pack

New for Lorcana and exclusive to local game stores from 8 May. Contains 6 booster packs, one of six possible Traveler promo cards, a cardboard deck box, four lore tracker dice, a special set item, and a rules sheet. Expected retail approximately AU$55 to AU$65. Only available in English.

The prerelease pack is the only way to get the Traveler promo cards at launch. If you are a collector who wants the full promo set, attending a prerelease event at your local game store is the only path to these cards before they appear on the secondary market.

### Themed Accessories

Playmats featuring Merida and Goofy are confirmed for Wilds Unknown. Standard Lorcana accessories including card sleeves and deck boxes are expected alongside release. These will be listed on the C3 shop page as ASINs are confirmed on Amazon AU.
