Where the first Eldraine set leaned into Arthurian legend and storybook aesthetics, Wilds leans into Brothers Grimm — fractured fairy tales, enchanted forests, and characters twisted by magic.

The set is notable for two things: an unusually strong main set with multiple competitive format staples, and the Enchanting Tales bonus sheet that appears in every single pack. The combination makes Wilds of Eldraine one of the most discussed sets for EV among Australian players.

## What Is Wilds of Eldraine and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Wilds of Eldraine contains 30 Enchanting Tales bonus cards — reprints of powerful enchantments from across MTG history printed in a special fairy tale illustration style. These cards appear in every pack alongside the regular set cards. The bonus sheet includes Sylvan Library, Parallel Lives, Smothering Tithe, Rhystic Study, and Doubling Season — Commander staples that were worth AU$20-80+ before this reprinting and still hold significant value in their special treatment versions. The Play Booster box contains 36 packs with 14 cards each.

## Chase Cards and What to Look For

From the Enchanting Tales bonus sheet: Sylvan Library, Parallel Lives, Smothering Tithe, and Rhystic Study in special treatment are the top targets. From the main set: Beseech the Mirror is a competitive powerhouse worth AU$15-30+. Virtue of Persistence and Up the Beanstalk are Pioneer and Modern staples. Faerie Mastermind is a competitive mythic with ongoing demand. The full Virtue cycle representing each colour has maintained Commander interest.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the Enchanting Tales bonus sheet?**
The Enchanting Tales is a 30-card bonus set of enchantment reprints that appears in every Wilds of Eldraine pack. Each pack contains one Enchanting Tales card alongside the regular set cards. The bonus sheet includes Commander staples like Sylvan Library, Parallel Lives, Smothering Tithe, and Rhystic Study in special fairy tale illustration treatments. These cards can be worth AU$5-60+ depending on the card and treatment.

**Is Wilds of Eldraine worth opening in Australia?**
Wilds of Eldraine has one of the strongest EV profiles of any 2023 standard set due to the Enchanting Tales bonus sheet. Every pack contains a guaranteed bonus card that could be worth AU$0.50 or AU$60+ depending on which Enchanting Tales card appears. At AU$180 for 36 packs the average EV is above most standard sets. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current assessment.

**What are the best Enchanting Tales cards to pull?**
Sylvan Library, Parallel Lives, Smothering Tithe, Rhystic Study, and Doubling Season are the top five Enchanting Tales targets by AU secondary market value. Foil or special extended art versions of these cards command significant premiums above the regular Enchanting Tales price.

**How does Wilds of Eldraine compare to other 2023 sets?**
Wilds of Eldraine is the strongest EV standard set of 2023 ahead of March of the Machine (which has its own Multiverse Legends bonus sheet) and The Lost Caverns of Ixalan. Lord of the Rings as a Universes Beyond set from 2023 also competes for top position, but among standard-legal sets Wilds leads.
