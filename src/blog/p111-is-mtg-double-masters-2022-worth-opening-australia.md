## What Is Double Masters 2022 and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Double Masters 2022 is a premium reprint set not sold in big box stores, designed specifically to bring down the prices of expensive Commander and Legacy staples. Every pack guarantees two rares or mythics, meaning a 24-pack box contains approximately 48 rares or mythics rather than the standard 24. The set reprints Dockside Extortionist (previously AU$80+ per copy), Wrenn and Six, Imperial Seal, Kozilek Butcher of Truth, and dozens of other high-demand cards. Borderless and textless treatment versions of key cards are available at premium rates.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does the double rare guarantee work in Double Masters 2022?**
Every pack in Double Masters 2022 contains two rare or mythic slots instead of the standard one. This means a 24-pack box contains approximately 48 rares and mythics rather than the standard 24. The mythic rate within those slots is approximately 1 in 4 packs for the first rare slot and lower for the second, giving roughly 6 mythics per box.

**Is Double Masters 2022 worth opening in Australia?**
Double Masters 2022 has one of the strongest EV profiles of any MTG product available on Amazon AU. The double rare guarantee and density of Legacy and Commander staples make it above-average value at AU$180. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current personalised assessment.

**What happened to Dockside Extortionist after the reprinting?**
Dockside Extortionist was worth AU$80-100+ before Double Masters 2022 brought it to AU$30-60+. The reprinting significantly reduced the price but the card remains one of the most powerful Commander cards ever printed and continues to hold strong secondary market value. It is still a significant pull from any box.

**Are borderless versions worth significantly more in 2X2?**
Yes. Borderless and textless treatment versions of key cards in Double Masters 2022 command premiums of AU$10-50+ above the regular card price depending on the card. Borderless Dockside Extortionist, Wrenn and Six, and Kozilek Butcher of Truth are particularly sought after in the AU collector market.
