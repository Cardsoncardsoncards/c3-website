The most valuable Riftbound cards in Australia sit across all four sets. Origins holds the foundational champion cards, Spiritforged has format-defining support pieces, Unleashed has the most actively traded singles due to current competitive relevance, and Vendetta pricing is pending launch. ## How Riftbound Card Value Works

Riftbound card values in Australia are driven by three factors acting simultaneously: competitive demand from tournament players, collector demand for premium and alternate-art versions of popular champions, and scarcity based on rarity and set availability. Understanding which factor is driving a specific card's price helps you judge whether that price is sustainable. A card priced high purely from competitive demand can drop rapidly if the meta shifts. A card priced high from collector demand tends to hold value more steadily.

## Most Valuable Origins Cards

Origins cards hold value for two distinct reasons. High-rarity champion cards and their alternate-art variants carry collector significance as the game's first-printed versions of each champion. Support cards from Origins that remain competitive staples across all four sets hold competitive demand from players building current-format decks. The combination of both factors on the same card creates the highest value floor. Check current Origins prices at [/cards/riftbound/sets/origins](/cards/riftbound/sets/origins).

## Most Valuable Spiritforged Cards

Spiritforged introduced new champions whose competitive performance was established through the following year of tournament play. Champions from Spiritforged that proved themselves in the Unleashed-era competitive scene hold consistent AU value. Support cards from Spiritforged that enable current top-tier strategies are the secondary targets. Check Spiritforged prices at [/cards/riftbound/sets/spiritforged](/cards/riftbound/sets/spiritforged).

## Most Valuable Unleashed Cards

Unleashed is where the most active price movement happens in Australian Riftbound.
