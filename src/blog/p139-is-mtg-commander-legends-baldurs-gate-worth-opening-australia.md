## What Is Commander Legends: Baldur's Gate and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Commander Legends: Baldur's Gate uses the same Commander Draft structure as the 2020 Commander Legends set — every pack guarantees two legendary creatures for Commander Draft play. The Draft Booster box contains 24 packs. The Initiative mechanic allows the Initiative-holder to venture through the Undercity dungeon, receiving bonuses at each room and the powerful completion effect at the end. Foil Etched versions of legendary creatures are the premium collector versions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Commander Legends: Baldur's Gate worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$170 for 24 packs with two legends guaranteed per pack, Baldur's Gate offers reasonable EV for Commander players. The DnD crossover appeal from Baldur's Gate 3's success and the Initiative mechanic's competitive relevance sustain demand. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current personalised assessment.

**What is the Initiative mechanic?**
The Initiative is a designation held by one player at a time — similar to the Monarch mechanic. The Initiative holder ventures through the Undercity dungeon at the start of their turn, receiving room bonuses. You take the Initiative by casting cards with the Initiative keyword or by attacking the current Initiative holder. The completion bonus (from clearing the dungeon) is powerful enough that Initiative cards saw Legacy and Vintage tournament play.

**Did Baldur's Gate 3 affect the MTG set's value?**
Yes. Baldur's Gate 3 released in 2023 and became a massive critical and commercial success, significantly expanding the audience familiar with and interested in the Forgotten Realms setting. This retroactively boosted interest in the MTG Commander Legends: Baldur's Gate set from 2022. Characters like Raphael and Astarion from BG3 do not appear in the MTG set (it predates BG3) but the fanbase overlap drove general interest.

**How does this compare to Commander Legends 2020 for EV?**
Commander Legends 2020 has Jewelled Lotus as its headline card — a significantly higher-value individual pull than anything in Baldur's Gate. The 2020 set has slightly higher average EV due to Jewelled Lotus and Opposition Agent as broader-demand cards. Baldur's Gate has stronger crossover appeal from the DnD and video game community. Both are worth opening for Commander players.
