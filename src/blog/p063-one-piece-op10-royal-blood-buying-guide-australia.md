## What Is OP-10 Royal Blood?

Royal Blood is the tenth main booster set for the One Piece Card Game. It covers the Punk Hazard and Dressrosa arcs of the manga and introduces six new leader cards including Caesar Clown and Smoker. The set is most notable for two things. First, it is set in Dressrosa, home to Trafalgar Law and Donquixote Doflamingo, two of the most popular characters in the One Piece series globally. Second, it introduced the Manga Rare rarity type to the English OPTCG format, featuring original manga panel artwork at pull rates far lower than any other rarity in the game.

The combination of popular characters and a new ultra-rare card type produced one of the highest-value individual cards the One Piece TCG has ever printed.

## What Makes OP-10 Different from Other OPTCG Sets

Three things distinguish Royal Blood from the broader OPTCG catalogue.

**The Manga Rare rarity.** This is the first English OPTCG set to include a Manga Rare. The artwork is drawn directly from Eiichiro Oda's original manga panels, giving it a completely different visual character from standard TCG card art. The pull rate of approximately 0.55% per pack (1 in 182 packs) makes it one of the rarest individual card types in any mainstream TCG currently in print.

**The Dressrosa character lineup.** Trafalgar Law, Donquixote Doflamingo, Donquixote Rosinante (Corazon), Caesar Clown, and the Dressrosa-arc supporting cast are among the most popular characters in the One Piece franchise globally. Sets featuring beloved characters sustain secondary market demand better than sets featuring less popular arcs.

**Above-retail box expected value.** More than a year after release, OP-10 booster displays return expected value above the retail price based on the combined value of cards you statistically pull per box. This is rare. Most TCG sets drop below retail expected value within three to six months of release.
