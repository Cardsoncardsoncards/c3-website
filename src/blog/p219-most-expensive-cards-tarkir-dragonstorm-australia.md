## Most Valuable Tarkir: Dragonstorm Cards

### Legendary Dragon Cycle

Tarkir: Dragonstorm features a cycle of legendary Dragons associated with each of the five Tarkir clans. These legendary creatures are all potential Commander choices with unique abilities.

**Top-value Dragon Legendaries**: the most popular legendary Dragons from the set (measured by EDHREC inclusion rates and secondary market demand) typically sit in the AU$15 to AU$50 range. Alternate art and foil versions command premiums of 2x to 5x the standard version.

Check specific card prices at [C3 MTG Card Vault](/cards/mtg).

### Dragon Tribal Payoffs

Cards that reward controlling Dragons or casting Dragon spells see broad Commander play because Dragon tribal is one of the most popular Commander themes. High-playability Dragon payoffs from Tarkir: Dragonstorm typically maintain AU$10 to AU$30 price points.

### New Format Staples

Sets set in established planes like Tarkir often produce unexpected format staples — efficient spells or utility creatures that find homes across multiple strategies. These cards may not be obvious at launch but grow in value as players discover applications.

## The Special Treatments Premium

Tarkir: Dragonstorm features:

**Showcase frames**: clan-themed illustrated borders reflecting the aesthetics of each Tarkir clan (Abzan, Jeskai, Sultai, Mardu, Temur).

**Borderless alternate art**: full-bleed art for the most prestigious cards. Top-end borderless versions of the legendary Dragons are the primary collector targets.

**Foil treatment**: standard foil on all rarities.

The showcase frame premium over standard frame is typically 2x to 4x for desirable cards. Borderless versions of the top Mythics can reach AU$40 to AU$100+ in foil.

## Opening vs Buying Singles

Tarkir: Dragonstorm Play Boosters retail in Australia at approximately AU$9 to AU$10 each, with booster boxes at approximately AU$200 to AU$240.

For the legendary Dragons and top Mythics: singles are more cost-efficient than opening boxes hoping to hit them.

For the opening experience and bulk collection building: boxes remain worthwhile as an entertainment purchase.
