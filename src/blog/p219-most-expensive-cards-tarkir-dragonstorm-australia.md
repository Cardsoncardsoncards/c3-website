## The Most Valuable Cards in Tarkir: Dragonstorm

### Ojutai, Soul of Winter

The centrepiece of the Ojutai clan, this legendary Dragon has sustained Commander demand due to its combination of evasion, card advantage, and a powerful triggered ability. White-blue players building around flying creatures made this one of the most-included legendaries from the set in the months following release.

**Why it holds value:** Commander staple. Consistent demand from Azorius players. Difficult to reprint outside a Masters set.

**Current AUD pricing:** Check [live price at C3](/cards/mtg/ojutai-soul-of-winter).

### Silumgar, the Drifting Death

The Silumgar clan Dragon offers a combination of hexproof and a board-wipe trigger that Commander players have consistently valued. Grixis and Dimir Commander decks building around Dragons or deathtouch effects run this card.

**Why it holds value:** Hexproof makes it resilient to targeted removal answers, which means it shows up and stays on board. That consistency translates to sustained casual demand.

**Current AUD pricing:** Check [live price at C3](/cards/mtg/silumgar-the-drifting-death).

### Kolaghan, the Storm's Fury

The Mardu Dragon. Kolaghan rewards wide board states and Dragon tribal strategies with a Dash ability that enables surprise attacks. Mardu Dragon Commander builds lean on this card as a curve-topper.

**Current AUD pricing:** Check [live price at C3](/cards/mtg/kolaghan-the-storms-fury).

### Atarka, World Render

The Temur Dragon with double strike and a triggered attack boost for all other Dragons. One of the most popular Dragons for Dragon tribal Commander decks regardless of which set it appears in. Tarkir: Dragonstorm gave the card a fresh printing that temporarily reduced price before demand stabilised.

**Current AUD pricing:** Check [live price at C3](/cards/mtg/atarka-world-render).

### Dromoka, the Eternal

The Abzan Dragon with bolster and a triggered counter distribution effect. Commander players building lifegain or counter-based strategies include this regularly.

**Current AUD pricing:** Check [live price at C3](/cards/mtg/dromoka-the-eternal).
