## Riftbound 2026

Riftbound has the most active competitive calendar of any new TCG in 2026. Three set releases and seven Regional Qualifiers are confirmed.

**Set Releases:**

- Released: Origins (Set 1), October 2025 launch set
- Released: Spiritforged (Set 2), February 2026
- Released: Unleashed (Set 3), May 8, 2026. 220+ cards, Ultimate rarity, XP/Hunt/Ambush mechanics
- July 31: Vendetta (Set 4), first set with simultaneous global English and Chinese release
- October 23: Radiance (Set 5), final 2026 set, features Ekko and Seraphine

**Regional Qualifiers and Major Events:**

- 15-17 May: RQ Sydney, Sydney Showground, Olympic Park NSW. First APAC RQ.
- 29-31 May: RQ Vancouver, Vancouver Convention Centre, Canada
- 12-14 June: RQ Utrecht, Jaarbeurs Utrecht, Netherlands
- 19-21 June: RQ Hartford, Connecticut Convention Center, USA
- 24-30 July: Vendetta Pre-Rift Week, global LGS events
- 21-23 August: RQ Barcelona, Spain
- 4-6 September: RQ Singapore. Second APAC RQ, relevant for Australian competitors.
- 21 September: Radiance Preview Season Begins
- 23 October: Radiance (Set 5) releases globally
- 25-27 October: RQ Los Angeles, final 2026 Regional Qualifier

Read the full RQ Sydney event guide at [/blog/p150-riftbound-rq-sydney-2026-guide](/blog/p150-riftbound-rq-sydney-2026-guide).
