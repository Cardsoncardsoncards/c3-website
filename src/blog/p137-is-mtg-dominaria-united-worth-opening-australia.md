## What Is Dominaria United and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Dominaria United begins the Phyrexian invasion arc on Dominaria, the original MTG plane. The set features the Legends Retro Frame bonus sheet — 32 legendary creature reprints printed in the classic old-border card frame appearing in every pack. Key reprints include Liliana of the Veil, Sheoldred Whispering One, and other high-demand legendary creatures. The Play Booster box contains 36 packs. Kicker is the returning mechanic. The invasion framing introduces new Phyrexian-corrupted versions of classic Dominaria characters.

## Chase Cards and What to Look For

Liliana of the Veil is the headline reprint — a three-mana planeswalker that has been a Modern and Legacy staple since original Innistrad (2011). Worth AU$15-35+ in Dominaria United. Legends Retro Frame treatment of Liliana commands additional collector premiums. Sheoldred the Apocalypse is the most valuable card from the main set — a four-mana 4/5 black legendary creature that gains you two life when you draw and deals two damage to opponents when they draw. It was worth AU$40-80+ at peak and remains AU$20-50+. Shivan Devastator and Temporary Lockdown are competitive staples.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the Legends Retro Frame bonus sheet?**
The Legends Retro Frame is a 32-card bonus set of legendary creature reprints printed in the classic old-border pre-8th Edition card frame appearing in every Dominaria United pack. Values range from low-demand legendaries worth AU$0.50 to Liliana of the Veil worth AU$15-35+ in the retro frame treatment.

**Is Dominaria United worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$160 for 36 packs, Dominaria United offers moderate EV with Sheoldred as the main set star and Liliana as the Legends Retro Frame headline. The Legends sheet creates guaranteed retro frame content per pack. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current assessment.

**Why is Sheoldred the Apocalypse so valuable?**
Sheoldred is a four-mana 4/5 with Deathtouch that gains you two life whenever you draw a card and deals two damage to opponents whenever they draw. In Standard and Pioneer, the life gain and damage output create an insurmountable advantage in long games. It was worth AU$40-80+ during peak Standard play and remains one of the most impactful creatures from the 2022 era.

**How does Dominaria United compare to The Brothers' War for EV?**
Both are 2022 sets at AU$160 with bonus sheet mechanics. Dominaria United has Sheoldred as a significantly more valuable main set card and Liliana as a stronger Legends Retro Frame target versus Brothers' War artifacts. Dominaria United has a slight EV edge. Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty from earlier in 2022 outperforms both.
