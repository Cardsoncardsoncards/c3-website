## What Is Assassin's Creed and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Assassin's Creed is a smaller-format Universes Beyond product using the Beyond Booster format — 24 packs per box rather than the standard 36. The set spans the full Assassin's Creed game history from the original 2007 game through Odyssey and Origins. Each protagonist appears as a legendary creature with Assassin's Creed-flavoured abilities — Hidden Blade, Eagle Vision, Parkour movement. Serialised versions of key characters are the premium collector targets. The IP fanbase in Australia is enormous given the gaming community's size.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why does Assassin's Creed have only 24 packs per box?**
Assassin's Creed uses the Beyond Booster format — a smaller product designed for Universes Beyond crossover sets. The 24-pack format reduces the total box cost relative to a 36-pack at the same per-pack price, but at AU$180 the per-pack cost is actually higher than standard sets. This is important for EV calculations — you get fewer packs for the same money.

**Is MTG Assassin's Creed worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$180 for 24 packs, Assassin's Creed has a higher per-pack cost than standard sets. Whether it is worth opening depends on your purchase price and the pull rate of chase mythics. The IP demand keeps single prices elevated. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current personalised assessment.

**What Assassin's Creed games are represented in the set?**
The set spans the full franchise history: Assassin's Creed (2007) with Altair, ACII through Revelations with Ezio, ACIII with Connor, ACIV with Edward Kenway, Origins with Bayek, Odyssey with Kassandra, and Valhalla with Eivor. The broader the franchise representation the wider the potential buyer audience across different game eras.

**Are serialised Assassin's Creed cards worth significantly more?**
Yes. Serialised cards in Assassin's Creed are numbered limited editions — for example, Ezio serialised 001/500. These are exceptionally rare and worth significantly above the non-serialised market rate. A serialised Ezio or Altair is one of the most valuable pulls from any Assassin's Creed box.
