## Which Pokemon Booster Box Should You Buy in 2026?

If you're buying sealed Pokemon product in Australia right now, the Scarlet and Violet era is where you want to focus. Older Sword and Shield boxes are either reprinted into the ground or sitting at premiums that make the maths difficult for new buyers.

This guide covers the current SV sets available on Amazon AU, ranked by sealed value retention, reprint risk, and how easy they are to find at MSRP.

## Current Set Rankings

### Tier 1: Prismatic Evolutions

The highest demand set of the SV era. Ultra-rare pull rates, Eevee-lution focus, and a collector base that doesn't care about MSRP. If you find it at retail price, buy it.

### Tier 2: Surging Sparks

Strong pull rates, Pikachu-themed set with mainstream appeal. More accessible than Prismatic Evolutions and easier to find at near-MSRP.

### Tier 3: Stellar Crown

Solid mid-tier set. Good for players, reasonable for collectors. Less chase pull pressure means boxes sit closer to MSRP consistently.

## What to Avoid

**Older SV sets at premium prices.** Paradox Rift and Obsidian Flames have been heavily reprinted. Unless you find them at MSRP or below, the resale upside is limited.

**Bundles and ETBs as investments.** These are designed for players and have worse EV than booster boxes. Buy them if you want to play, not if you want to resell.

## Where to Buy

Amazon AU is the most reliable source for MSRP pricing on mainstream sets. eBay is worth checking for discontinued sets but expect to pay a premium.
