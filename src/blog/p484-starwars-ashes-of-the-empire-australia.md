## What Is Star Wars Unlimited?

Star Wars Unlimited is the official Star Wars Trading Card Game published by Fantasy Flight Games. It launched in 2024 and has built a steadily growing player base in Australia, supported by an organised play structure called Planetary Qualifiers and a global Galactic Championship series. The game uses a base-attack system that differs from most TCGs, with players choosing to attack the opponent's leader or damage their base directly each turn.

Ashes of the Empire is a new expansion set. For current Star Wars Unlimited card prices, use our [Star Wars Unlimited card price tracker](/cards/starwars).

## Is Star Wars Unlimited Worth Getting Into in 2026?

Yes, if you are drawn to the IP or looking for a game that is still in its growth phase. Star Wars Unlimited is past the initial launch hype but not yet fully saturated. The game has maintained organised play investment from Fantasy Flight Games and the card pool is deep enough that multiple archetypes are viable, but not so large that new players are priced out.

The comparison to other TCGs at the 2-year mark: Star Wars Unlimited is in a healthier position than some games at this age, mostly because the IP appeal draws in players who might not otherwise look at TCGs. Ashes of the Empire releasing with active Planetary Qualifier support throughout July means the competitive community is engaged.
