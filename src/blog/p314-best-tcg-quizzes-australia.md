## Why TCG Quizzes Are More Useful Than They Sound

The TCG hobby involves a lot of decisions that benefit from honest self-assessment. Which game should you play? Which deck archetype suits how you think? Are you collecting for love of the cards or with an eye on resale value? What era of Pokemon do you actually belong in?

Most TCG content answers these questions with generic advice that applies to everyone and therefore applies to no one. A quiz forces specificity. You answer questions about your actual situation, budget, preferences, and play style, and get a recommendation calibrated to your specific profile rather than the average player.

The C3 quizzes were built with Australian players specifically in mind. Budget ranges are in AUD, community size recommendations account for Australian player bases, and product suggestions reference what is actually available in this market.

## How the Quiz Results Work

Every quiz gives you a named result with a two-sentence explanation of why it suits you, a link to the relevant game hub or beginner guide, and a suggested starter action. Results can be shared on Twitter, Reddit, WhatsApp, and Facebook directly from the result screen.

The site tracks how many people have taken each quiz and what the most common result is, which is displayed on each quiz tile at [/play](/play). These are real numbers from actual quiz completions, not placeholder figures.
