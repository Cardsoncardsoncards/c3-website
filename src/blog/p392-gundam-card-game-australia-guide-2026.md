## What Is the Gundam Card Game?

The Gundam Card Game (officially Gundam Card Game, published by Bandai) is a new TCG designed around the Mobile Suit Gundam franchise spanning forty-plus years of anime, including the original Mobile Suit Gundam (0079), Zeta Gundam, Gundam Wing, SEED, 00, Iron-Blooded Orphans, and beyond.

Players build decks around a chosen faction and mobile suit lineup, deploying Mobile Suits as Units and using Pilot cards to enhance their capabilities. The game captures the Gundam franchise's signature aesthetic of large-scale mobile suit combat.

The Gundam Card Game launched globally in late 2024 and the Australian market is in its early growth phase. Bandai's investment in launch events and organised play infrastructure signals a committed push for the game internationally.

## Most Valuable Gundam Card Game Cards in Australia

**Super Rare (SR) and Secret Rare (SEC) versions** of iconic Mobile Suits are the primary collector targets. The specific suit and series determines value:

**RX-78-2 Gundam** (original series) cards at premium rarity represent the franchise's most iconic mech. Any high-rarity version of the original Gundam commands collector premiums from fans who grew up with the original series.

**Wing Gundam Zero (EW)** and other Gundam Wing Mobile Suits have strong nostalgia demand in Australia, where Gundam Wing was many Australian fans' first Gundam exposure through Cartoon Network.

**SEED-era Mobile Suits** (Strike Gundam, Freedom Gundam, Justice Gundam) have dedicated fan communities from the SEED and SEED Destiny generation.

**Iron-Blooded Orphans** (IBO) era cards have strong demand from newer fans who came to Gundam through the 2015 series.

**Tournament promo cards** from Gundam Card Game championship events have genuine scarcity as the competitive infrastructure develops.

## Entry Cost and Starting the Gundam Card Game

**Starter Decks** for specific Gundam factions cost roughly AU$20 to AU$35 and provide a complete playable deck with iconic Mobile Suits from that series.

**Booster boxes** run AU$100 to AU$130. Value concentrates in SR and SEC cards of popular series Mobile Suits.

**Singles** through eBay AU and specialist stores are the most cost-effective path for targeted deck building.

## The Australian Gundam Community

The Gundam Card Game is in early community development in Australia. Bandai's organised play infrastructure is being established with store championships beginning to run in major cities.

**The broader Gundam hobby community** in Australia provides a natural audience for the card game. Gundam model kit (Gunpla) builders and anime fans are the natural first adopters.

**Bandai's support commitment** is evident from the game's launch investment. As one of the largest toy and hobby companies globally, Bandai's financial backing for the Gundam Card Game gives it stronger long-term prospects than independent TCG launches.
