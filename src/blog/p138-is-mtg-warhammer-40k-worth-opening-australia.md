## What Is Warhammer 40,000 and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

MTG Warhammer 40,000 covers the four major factions: the Imperium of Man (Space Marines), the Necrons, the Tyranids, and Chaos. Each faction has an associated Commander deck with thematic mechanics. The Collector Booster box contains 12 packs with higher foil density than standard products. The Surge Foil treatment creates an iridescent colour-shifting foil effect — more visually dynamic than traditional silver foiling. Serialised versions of faction leaders are the most valuable collector targets in the product.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is the Warhammer 40K Collector Booster worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$320 for 12 packs, the per-pack cost is high. EV depends heavily on hitting Surge Foil versions of high-demand faction leaders. The Australian Warhammer community drives collector demand that standard MTG metrics do not capture. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current personalised assessment.

**What is Surge Foil treatment?**
Surge Foil is a premium foiling treatment used in Warhammer 40K Collector Boosters that creates an iridescent, colour-shifting effect when the card is tilted under light. Unlike traditional silver foiling, Surge Foil shifts through multiple colours creating a dynamic visual effect. It is the most visually striking foil treatment in this product and commands significant premiums over non-foil versions.

**Do I need the Commander decks to use the Collector Booster cards?**
No. Collector Booster cards are functionally identical to their Commander deck counterparts — they are premium foil versions of the same cards. You can use Collector Booster Surge Foil cards in any Commander deck running the corresponding faction theme without owning the precon deck. They are fully tournament legal in Commander.

**Which Warhammer 40K faction has the most valuable MTG cards?**
Space Marines generally command the highest premiums in the AU market due to the faction's popularity with Australian Warhammer players. The Emperor of Mankind in Surge Foil is typically the highest-value pull. Necron cards also perform strongly due to the faction's distinctive visual aesthetic. Chaos faction cards (Abaddon) round out the top tier.
