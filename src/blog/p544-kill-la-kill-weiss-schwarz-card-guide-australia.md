# Kill la Kill in Weiss Schwarz: Ryuko, Satsuki and the Clothes Trait

Kill la Kill brings Ryuko Matoi and her sentient uniform Senketsu into Weiss Schwarz, built around confirmed 《Clothes》 and 《Family》 character traits that community deck guides document extensively. If you already play and want the archetype detail, jump ahead. If you are newer to Weiss Schwarz, the primer below covers the core rules, with the full mechanics glossary linked at the end.

## The set at a glance

Kill la Kill spans 2 tracked releases, the original set (26 September 2014) and a Power Up Set (March 2023), totalling 179 cards.

## Quick primer: how Weiss Schwarz works

Every deck is 50 cards split across colours, cards get stronger as your clock (a face-down pile built from damage taken) grows, climax cards (up to 8 per deck) combo with character abilities on the right turn, and you can run up to four copies of any named card. For the full breakdown, read our [complete rules glossary](/blog/how-weiss-schwarz-works-rules-deck-building-australia).

## Documented Ryuko and Satsuki Archetypes

Community deck guides confirm at least two distinct documented builds. A dedicated Ryuko build runs almost entirely her own card line supported by Senketsu as a marker target, built around her level 3 combo, "Graduating from Sailor Uniform," which grants a significant power boost with a documented follow-up effect at level 2 or higher that disrupts the opponent's deck order. A separate documented build, a "Satsuki and Ryuko sister deck," has been played and reported as tournament-winning in community deck tech videos, combining both characters' card lines around their shared story arc. The confirmed Clothes and Family traits give supporting synergy underneath either approach, since cards check for characters sharing these traits to trigger power boosts.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Should I build a Ryuko-only deck or a Satsuki and Ryuko sister deck?**
Both are documented community builds, a Ryuko-only build centres on her level 0 through level 3 combo line with Senketsu as support, while a sister-focused build combines Ryuko and Satsuki's card lines.

**What do the Clothes and Family traits actually do?**
They are confirmed character traits that certain cards check for directly, granting power boosts when you have other characters sharing these traits.

**Is all Power Up Set material available in English?**
Community sources note some Power Up Set cards are only available in Japanese, worth confirming card by card before assuming a fully English collection is possible.

**Do older Kill la Kill sets remain legal in tournament play?**
Weiss Schwarz does not use a rotating standard format, so older cards generally remain playable, though always check a specific event's rules before entering.

**Who are the confirmed Elite Four characters in this card pool?**
Inumuta, Jakuzure, Sanageyama, and Gamagoori are confirmed named characters representing the Elite Four from the source material, each with their own documented card lines in the confirmed catalogue.

**How does the four copy limit affect choosing between a Ryuko-only or sister-focused build?**
It reinforces committing to one documented build, since running four copies of key cards from both Ryuko's solo combo line and a broader sister-focused approach at once would spread your deck too thin to execute either reliably.
