Grixis (blue-black-red) is one of the most powerful three-colour combinations in Commander. It combines blue's card draw and counters, black's tutors and removal, and red's speed and wheels. The weakness is no green ramp and no white removal — but the colour combination is deep enough that many tables consider Grixis to be among the strongest.

## What Grixis Does Best

Drawing cards through multiple vectors simultaneously. Wheels (Wheel of Fortune, Windfall, Narset's Reversal) refuel hands. Black tutors find combo pieces. Blue counters protect them. Red provides the speed to execute before opponents stabilise.

## Top Grixis Commanders

### Inalla, Archmage Ritualist

Wizard tribal with a powerful trigger: whenever you cast a Wizard, you may pay 1 mana to create a hasty token copy of it until end of turn. In a deck full of powerful Wizards, this generates double the ETB triggers every turn.

**Current AU price**: approximately AU$3 to AU$8.

### Nekusar, the Mindrazer

Every player draws an extra card each turn. Every player takes 1 damage for each card drawn. In a deck full of wheel effects, Nekusar rapidly deals lethal damage to the entire table.

**Why it works**: punishes card draw (which everyone does), scales with wheel density.
**Current AU price**: approximately AU$4 to AU$10.

### Jeleva, Nephalia's Scourge

Exiles cards from the top of each player's library when she enters the battlefield, and allows you to cast opponent's instants and sorceries for free when she attacks.

**Current AU price**: approximately AU$3 to AU$8.

### Malcolm, Keen-Eyed Navigator / Breeches, Brazen Plunder (partner pair)

A popular partner pairing for pirate tribal. Malcolm generates Treasure tokens when Pirates deal combat damage. Breeches lets you cast cards exiled from opponents' libraries. Together they create a fast, theft-based strategy.

**Combined current AU price**: approximately AU$4 to AU$10 total.

### Yuriko, the Tiger's Shadow

The strongest Grixis commander in terms of raw power ceiling. Yuriko uses Ninjutsu (sneak past blockers) to enter unblocked, and deals damage equal to the mana value of the top card of your library to all opponents when she does. With high-mana-value cards at the top, this deals enormous damage to the entire table simultaneously.

**Current AU price**: approximately AU$15 to AU$30.
