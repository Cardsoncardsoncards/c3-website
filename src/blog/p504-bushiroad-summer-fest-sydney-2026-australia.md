## What Is Bushiroad Summer Fest?

Bushiroad Summer Fest is Bushiroad's annual flagship event series, running across multiple countries in Asia, Oceania, and beyond each year. The Sydney edition brings together players of all three major Bushiroad TCGs for a Regional Championship weekend that functions as one of the most important qualification events in the Bushiroad organised play calendar.

Regional Championships at Bushiroad Summer Fest award ranking points and invitations to higher-level events including World Championships for each respective game. The competitive stakes are real for players pursuing the top of the Bushiroad organised play ladder.

Bankstown Sports Club is one of the largest event venues in Western Sydney and a familiar location for major TCG events in NSW. It is located in Bankstown, approximately 45 minutes from Sydney CBD by train on the T3 Bankstown Line from Central Station.
