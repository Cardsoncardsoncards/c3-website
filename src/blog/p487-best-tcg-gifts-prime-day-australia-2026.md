## If You Have No Idea What Game They Play

Start here. The three most recognisable TCGs in Australia right now are Pokemon, Disney Lorcana, and Magic: The Gathering. Pick based on who you are buying for.

### Pokemon: Scarlet and Violet Elite Trainer Box

Best for: Kids aged 8 and up, teenagers, adults who played Pokemon as kids | Budget: AU$75 to AU$90

An Elite Trainer Box is the safest TCG gift you can buy for a Pokemon fan at any age. It comes with 9 booster packs, sleeves, dice, a coin, and a promo card, all themed around a specific set. It is a complete package, it looks impressive when unwrapped, and it gives the recipient enough cards to actually play with. If in doubt about which set to choose, pick a current Scarlet and Violet ETB and you will not go wrong.

### Disney Lorcana: Illumineer's Trove

Best for: Disney fans, adults, collectors | Budget: AU$80 to AU$110

If you are buying for a Disney fan who does not necessarily play card games, Lorcana is the entry point. The Illumineer's Trove is Lorcana's premium bundle product, containing booster packs and a storage box with Disney artwork. It reads as a collectible as much as a card game product. Parents and adults who appreciate Disney as a brand will immediately understand the appeal. No knowledge of card games required.

Search Lorcana Illumineer's Trove on Amazon AU

### MTG: Commander Deck (Marvel Super Heroes)

Best for: Adults, Marvel fans, existing MTG players | Budget: AU$55 to AU$75

MTG Marvel Super Heroes released 26 June. The Commander decks feature Marvel characters as Magic cards. If you are buying for someone who likes Marvel and you suspect they might enjoy a card game, a Marvel Commander deck is a strong gift. It is ready to play out of the box, the Marvel branding does a lot of the explaining for you, and it sits at a price point that works as a birthday or Christmas gift. A Prime Day discount of AU$10 to AU$15 on a Commander deck is realistic.
