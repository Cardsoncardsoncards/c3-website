Mono-black is one of the most powerful single-colour options in Commander. It has access to the best tutors in the format (Demonic Tutor, Vampiric Tutor, Diabolic Intent), strong creature recursion, and multiple combo lines that work entirely within one colour.

## What Mono-Black Does Best

Tutoring for exactly what you need, recurring creatures from the graveyard, drawing cards through life payments, and generating mana through rituals and black-specific artifacts. Black also has some of the strongest "edict" removal (Dictate of Erebos, Grave Pact) that scales with token strategies.

## Top Mono-Black Commanders

### Sheoldred, Whispering One (as Commander)

Sheoldred forces opponents to sacrifice a creature at the start of each of their upkeeps while you reanimate a creature from your graveyard at yours. A powerful reanimator engine that simultaneously disrupts and rebuilds.

**Current AU price**: varies by printing from AU$8 to AU$30+. Check at [C3](/cards/mtg).

### K'rrik, Son of Yawgmoth

K'rrik allows you to pay life instead of black mana for black spells. In a mono-black deck, this means most spells cost primarily life rather than mana. Combined with lifegain, K'rrik enables explosive turns that generate enormous card and board advantage.

**Why it works**: life as a resource, massive tempo gains, scales extremely well.
**Current AU price**: approximately AU$10 to AU$20.

### Ayara, First of Locthwain

Ayara drains opponents for 1 life and gains you 1 life whenever another black creature enters the battlefield. With an aristocrats-style build full of sacrifice outlets, tokens, and recursion, the damage output is substantial.

**Why it works**: passive drain engine, reliable card advantage, accessible price point.
**Current AU price**: approximately AU$2 to AU$6.

### Erebos, God of the Dead

Erebos stops opponents gaining life (a meta-relevant ability) and allows you to draw cards by paying 2 life and tapping. Also an indestructible 5/7 in a devotion-heavy black deck.

**Why it works**: life gain hate, consistent card draw, impressive stats when active.
**Current AU price**: approximately AU$4 to AU$12.

### Yawgmoth, Thran Physician

One of the most powerful mono-black commanders in the format. Yawgmoth pays one life to put a -1/-1 counter on a target creature (including your own, enabling combo with undying creatures) and draws a card. The combo lines available to Yawgmoth are efficient and consistent.

**Current AU price**: approximately AU$20 to AU$45. One of the higher-investment mono-black options.

## Budget Mono-Black Under AU$10

## Essential Cards for Mono-Black Commander

- **Demonic Tutor** (AU$20 to AU$45): the best tutor in Commander
- **Vampiric Tutor** (AU$30 to AU$60): instant-speed tutor
- **Phyrexian Arena** (AU$3 to AU$8): consistent card draw
