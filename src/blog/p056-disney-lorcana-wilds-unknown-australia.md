## Why Wilds Unknown Is a Big Deal

Eleven sets passed before a single Pixar character appeared in Disney Lorcana. That changes with Wilds Unknown.

Toy Story, The Incredibles, and Brave all make their Lorcana debut in this set. Woody and Buzz Lightyear are the headline characters from the key art. Mrs. Incredible, Jack-Jack, and Merida from Brave have also been confirmed. The addition of Pixar opens up the collector base significantly. Fans of these films who have never engaged with Lorcana before now have a direct reason to buy in.

This is not a small tweak to the game. It is a genuine expansion of the IP universe that Lorcana draws from, and Ravensburger has confirmed that Pixar's presence will continue throughout 2026 and beyond. Monsters, Inc. and Turning Red arrive in Q3 with Attack of the Vine, and Coco is confirmed for Q4.

## Release Dates

- **Prerelease at local game stores:** 8 May 2026

## Products Confirmed for Australia

**Booster Display (24 packs)**
The main sealed product. 24 booster packs per display, each pack containing 12 randomised cards with the standard Lorcana rarity breakdown of 6 commons, 3 uncommons, 2 rares or higher, and 1 foil of random rarity. Pricing will be broadly comparable to Winterspell displays, which retail around AU$100 to AU$130 depending on retailer.

**Illumineer's Trove**
Lorcana's equivalent of a bundle. Approximately AU$50 to AU$65 based on previous sets. Includes 8 booster packs plus storage accessories, deck boxes, and damage counters. Strong value entry point for players who want a manageable amount of product without committing to a full booster display.

**Two-Player Starter Set (Elastigirl / Buzz Lightyear)**
A brand new product format for Lorcana, replacing the single-player starter decks that have been the entry-point product since launch. The Two-Player Starter Set includes two complete 60-card decks (Amethyst/Ruby for Elastigirl and Amber/Emerald for Buzz Lightyear), foil promo cards, playmats, lore trackers, and tokens. MSRP is approximately AU$55 to AU$70. This is an excellent gift product and a genuine improvement over buying two separate starter decks.

**Prerelease Pack**
New for Lorcana and available at local game stores from 8 May. Contains 6 booster packs from the Wilds Unknown set, one of six possible Traveler promo cards, a cardboard deck box, four lore tracker dice, a special set-specific item, and a rules sheet. MSRP approximately AU$55 to AU$65. Only available in English for this release.

**Themed Accessories**
Playmats featuring Merida and Goofy are confirmed. Standard Lorcana accessories including card sleeves and deck boxes are expected alongside release.

## New Mechanics in Wilds Unknown

**Traveler Cycle**
The Traveler mechanic is the headline mechanical addition of Wilds Unknown. Traveler cards (like Ariel, Curious Traveler) have powerful abilities that trigger when they quest, but only if you have played another character that same turn. This rewards synergy-focused play and adds a strategic timing element to questing. The six Traveler cycle cards also appear as the promo cards inside prerelease packs, giving each a unique foil treatment.

**New Character Classification: Toy**
Buzz Lightyear's card introduces the new Toy character type, which is expected to have internal synergies within the Toy Story character group as more cards are revealed.

## Confirmed Characters

**Toy Story:** Woody, Buzz Lightyear (Amber/Emerald deck, two-player starter), Jessie (confirmed).
**The Incredibles:** Elastigirl / Mrs. Incredible (Amethyst/Ruby deck), Jack-Jack Parr, plus additional Incredible family members expected.
**Brave:** Merida, Formidable Archer (confirmed on booster pack artwork and playmat).
**Classic Disney:** Ariel (Traveler cycle), Gadget Hackwrench (Resourceful Mechanic), and additional classic characters across the 204-card set.

The full 204-card set includes 18 Epic cards, 18 Enchanted cards, and 2 Iconic cards alongside the main set.

## Who Should Buy Wilds Unknown

**Existing Lorcana collectors:** This set is a priority buy. The first Pixar cards in the game will anchor collector interest for years. Enchanted rarity versions of Woody, Buzz, Elastigirl, or Jack-Jack have obvious long-term collector appeal beyond the Lorcana player base.

**New players using this as an entry point:** The Two-Player Starter Set is now the best possible entry into Lorcana. Two complete decks, foil promos, and all the accessories needed to start playing immediately. It is the right product for someone who wants to learn the game with a friend.

**Disney and Pixar fans who do not play TCGs:** This is the set that creates a reason for Toy Story and Incredibles fans to engage with Lorcana as a product. The chase Enchanted versions of the most popular Pixar characters will be the crossover collectible item for 2026 for this audience.

**Players focused on deck building and competition:** The Traveler cycle adds new deckbuilding tools. Whether these cards become competitive staples depends on the full card list, which will be revealed progressively through April before the 15 May release.
