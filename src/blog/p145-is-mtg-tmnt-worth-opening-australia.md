## Chase Cards and What to Look For

Pizza-themed card variants (food tokens or card treatments incorporating the pizza motif) have strong novelty collector appeal. Any serialised version of a turtle legendary is an exceptional pull worth significantly above non-serialised prices.

**What colour combinations are the four turtles?**
The four turtles represent different colour philosophies reflecting their personalities: Leonardo (the disciplined leader) is typically White-Blue, Donatello (the inventor) is Blue-Green, Michelangelo (the fun-loving party dude) is Red-Green or Red-White, and Raphael (the aggressive loner) is Black-Red. **Can you play all four turtles in one Commander deck?**
Potentially yes, depending on the four-colour Commander options available. Individual turtle Commanders restrict you to two colours each. Check the actual card releases for the specific four-colour Commander options available in the set.
