## What Drives Price Differences Across Channels

Specialist hobby stores carry higher overheads than mass market retailers and price their Lorcana product accordingly. The premium you pay at a specialist store reflects their knowledge, community events, and curation. Many Lorcana players consider this worth supporting. If your goal is purely lowest price, mass market retailers and eBay are the right choice for sealed, and eBay or C3 are the right choice for singles.

**Import duty and GST** can apply to Lorcana orders from overseas stores. The European Lorcana community is large and some EU stores have competitive pricing, but shipping costs and potential import fees frequently negate any apparent savings for Australian buyers.

## Timing Your Purchases

Lorcana sealed product prices in Australia are typically lowest at:

**Retail promotions** (Big W, Target game sales, Black Friday, pre-Christmas).

**Older set clearances** when stores make room for new releases.

**Immediately after a set's initial hype period** when early demand has been met and supply is still available at retail.

Prices typically spike at new set launch and around major Lorcana event announcements, then settle lower as supply reaches the market.


## Buying Lorcana Singles in Bulk

Building a full Lorcana collection or assembling multiple competitive decks often involves buying several cards at once. Strategies for reducing per-card cost:

**Bundle purchases from single sellers** on eBay AU reduce postage costs. A seller who has multiple cards you need can combine them into one shipment. Many eBay AU Lorcana sellers offer combined postage or free postage above a spend threshold.

**Trading at locals.** The Lorcana community at Australian game stores is active and trading common and uncommon singles between players is standard practice. Cards you open but do not need for your ink colours can often be traded directly for cards you do need.

**Condition awareness.** Near Mint is the standard for secondary market Lorcana purchases in Australia. Lorcana cards can show edge wear and surface scratches more visibly than some other TCGs because of the card surface treatment. Request photos before buying Enchanted Rares or any singles above AU$20.
