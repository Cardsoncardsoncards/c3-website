## What Is Future Card Buddyfight?

Future Card Buddyfight was Bushiroad's TCG featuring players as fighters who pair with a Buddy Monster, building decks around specific "worlds" (Dragon World, Danger World, Magic World, etc.). The game ran from 2014 to 2020 with active sets and organised play in Australia.

Bushiroad ended new Buddyfight set production in 2020, which stopped the game's competitive development. However, a community of dedicated players and collectors remains in Australia, and the game can still be played with the existing card pool.

## Most Valuable Buddyfight Cards

**Legend Rares (Legend R)** are the highest rarity cards in Buddyfight and among the most visually impressive in the game's history. These feature enhanced foil treatments and premium artwork of significant characters from each world.

**Secret cards (Secret R and Buddy Rare)** are limited alternate art versions of popular cards. The Buddy Rare tier is specific to Buddyfight and represents high-pull-rarity versions of the game's most iconic Buddy Monsters.

**Dragon World cards** tend to command the highest premiums in the Australian Buddyfight secondary market because Dragon World was consistently the most played world during the game's competitive run.

**Early set Legend Rares** from the game's first few sets are particularly sought because supply has diminished over the years since the game ended active production.

**Promo cards from championships and events** that ran during the game's active years have genuine scarcity and collector demand among Buddyfight enthusiasts.

## Is Buddyfight Worth Starting in Australia in 2026?

**As a competitive TCG: No.** With no new sets or official organised play support since 2020, Buddyfight cannot be recommended as a competitive game to start for players who want current event access and ongoing publisher support.

**As a legacy casual game: Possibly.** If you connect with Buddyfight's world-based structure, have friends who play, or have existing nostalgia for the game, casual play is entirely viable. The card pool from the game's run is extensive and provides years of casual play content.

**As a collector: Niche but rewarding.** Buddyfight's dedicated collector community in Australia is small but active. Legend Rare and Buddy Rare versions of iconic monsters from beloved worlds hold genuine collector interest.
