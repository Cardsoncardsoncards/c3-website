**What are Case enchantments and how do they work?**
Cases are a new enchantment subtype that present a mystery to solve. Each Case has a To Solve condition — once you meet it, the Case upgrades to provide an ongoing bonus. They function like enchantments with a built-in progression system. Several Cases have become Commander staples because the solve conditions are achievable in multiplayer and the bonuses are impactful.

**Why is the Ravnica setting popular with MTG players?**
Ravnica is one of the most beloved MTG planes due to its ten two-colour guilds — Dimir, Azorius, Rakdos, Gruul, Selesnya, and the rest — each with distinct flavour, mechanics, and iconic characters. Commander players building guild-themed decks are drawn to Ravnica sets for commanders and thematic cards. The setting has appeared across multiple MTG sets since 2005.

**What is Delney Streetwise Lookout and why is it valuable?**
Delney is a Human Detective that doubles triggered abilities for creatures with two or less power. This creates powerful interactions with enter-the-battlefield triggers and combat triggers in Pioneer and Commander. It is a competitive staple in several Pioneer archetypes and a popular Commander card for creature-based combo strategies.
