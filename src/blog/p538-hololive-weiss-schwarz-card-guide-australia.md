## The set at a glance

Hololive spans 5 tracked releases, hololive production (13 May 2022), a Premium Booster (September 2022), Vol.2 (August 2023), a Weiss Schwarz Ambassadors Premium Set (August 2024), and a Summer Collection Premium Booster (August 2024), totalling 1,434 cards, well beyond the 3 sets sometimes assumed for this property.

## Quick primer: how Weiss Schwarz works

Every deck is 50 cards split across colours, cards get stronger as your clock (a face-down pile built from damage taken) grows, climax cards (up to 8 per deck) combo with character abilities on the right turn, and you can run up to four copies of any named card. For the full breakdown, read our [complete rules glossary](/blog/how-weiss-schwarz-works-rules-deck-building-australia).

## Multiple Generations, One Shared Trait

Hololive's confirmed card text builds around the 《hololive》 character trait, with cards regularly checking for other hololive characters on the board to trigger searches, power boosts, or additional draws. Community guides confirm each generation, 0th, 1st, 2nd, Gamers, and beyond, has its own thematic identity and tends to work best with cards from its own generation, while still allowing a mixed VTuber dream team deck for players who want to build across generations. Card numbers across the confirmed sets share the HOL prefix, meaning releases are designed to combine with each other under standard construction. For a new player, starting with a single generation's trial deck material, built around your favourite talent, is the recommended entry point before branching wider.
