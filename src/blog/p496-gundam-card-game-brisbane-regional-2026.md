## What Is the Gundam Card Game?

The Gundam Card Game is a constructed TCG published by Bandai based on the Mobile Suit Gundam anime franchise. Players build decks around mobile suits from the Gundam universe and compete in strategic battles using unit stats, pilot skills, and command cards.

The game draws on decades of Gundam IP spanning the original series, Zeta Gundam, Wing, SEED, Iron-Blooded Orphans, and other entries in the franchise. Knowledge of the Gundam IP enhances the experience but is not required to play competitively.
