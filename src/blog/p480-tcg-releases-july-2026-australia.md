## How to Shop the July Release Window

With this many releases in one month, the risk is spending too broadly and ending up with product you did not need. Here is a simple framework:

- Pick your main game first. What are you actually playing or collecting right now?
- Buy the new set for your main game on release day, not during Prime Day. New releases do not get sale pricing.
- Use Prime Day for older sealed product in your main game or a game you have been wanting to try.
- If you are buying for gifts or as a new player, use the [gift guide](/blog/best-tcg-gifts-prime-day-australia-2026) to focus on the right products.

For singles in any game, check our [card price tracker](/cards) before buying sealed. It is almost always cheaper to buy the specific cards you need as singles than to crack packs hoping for them.
