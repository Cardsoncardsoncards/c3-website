## Release Date and Global Parity

July 31, 2026. Vendetta releases in both English and Simplified Chinese simultaneously. This is the first time Riftbound has achieved true global release parity.

For Australian players this means no waiting. No watching international players crack packs for months before you can. No price spikes driven by import shortage. When Vendetta releases, you buy it at Australian retail on the same day as players in the US, Europe, and China.

This is a meaningful shift. Origins launched in China months before English. Spiritforged followed the same pattern. Unleashed had the gap close to weeks. Vendetta closes it entirely. Riot is treating the global player base as one market, which bodes well for the long-term health of the Australian competitive scene.

## What "Vendetta" Suggests

The name implies a reckoning, a score being settled. In League of Legends lore terms, this points toward champions driven by vengeance or rivalry. Riot has not officially confirmed which new Legends are in Vendetta as of the time of writing. Preview season is expected to start in late June 2026.

## What We Can Infer From the Pattern

Each Riftbound set has added 4-5 new Champion Legends to the roster. If Vendetta follows the same structure: 200+ new cards, 4-5 new Legends introducing new Domain combinations, new mechanics building on the XP/Hunt/Ambush foundation from Unleashed, and Overnumber alternate art variants as the chase cards.

No Ultimate Rare is confirmed. Riot has stated Ultimate is reserved for special occasions, not every set.

## What Australian Players Should Do Before Vendetta

**Play Unleashed while the meta is fresh.** RQ Sydney (May 15-17) and RQ Singapore (September 4-6) are both Unleashed-format events. Vendetta releases after Singapore, which means the entire APAC competitive season for 2026 is played in the Unleashed meta.

**Track which Unleashed cards hold value.** The singles market will shift when Vendetta previews begin in late June. Cards that are Unleashed-only and see play will maintain or grow their value.

**Pre-order if stock is limited.** Unleashed stock ran low at several Australian retailers quickly after release. If Vendetta follows the same pattern, pre-orders will be the safest way to guarantee product at retail price.

## The Bigger Picture

Three sets in one calendar year (Unleashed in May, Vendetta in July, Radiance in October) is an aggressive pace. The right approach for most Australian players is: stay engaged with the competitive meta, buy the singles you need for your specific deck, and evaluate sealed product on a set-by-set basis based on pull rates and chase card desirability.
