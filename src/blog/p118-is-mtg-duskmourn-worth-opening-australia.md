## What Is Duskmourn and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Duskmourn is set inside an infinitely large haunted house — a plane that is entirely one building. The set introduces the Rooms mechanic (double-faced enchantment rooms that unlock different parts of the house), Manifest Dread (playing cards face-down from the top of your library), and Eerie (a trigger mechanic for haunted events). The Nightmare Frame treatment applies a distorted horror aesthetic to special versions of cards. The Play Booster box contains 36 packs with 14 cards each.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Duskmourn worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$180, Duskmourn offers reasonable EV for a 2024 standard set. The horror theme drives collector demand for Nightmare Frame treatments and Commander players are drawn to the Horror tribal synergies. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current personalised assessment.

**What is the Nightmare Frame treatment?**
The Nightmare Frame is a special card treatment exclusive to Duskmourn that applies a distorted, horror-aesthetic version of the standard card frame. Cards receive warped borders, altered typography, and horror-themed visual elements. Nightmare Frame versions of mythics and popular rares command premiums in the AU secondary market from collectors drawn to the aesthetic.

**What are Rooms in Duskmourn?**
Rooms are a new card type in Duskmourn — double-faced enchantment cards representing different rooms in the haunted house. Each Room has two sides that can be unlocked independently by paying their respective costs. Once both sides are unlocked the Room provides a combined ongoing bonus. They function as enchantments that upgrade over time.

**How does Duskmourn compare to Bloomburrow for EV?**
Both sets are 2024 standard releases at AU$180 and sit in similar borderline EV territory. Bloomburrow has stronger tribal Commander appeal. Duskmourn has stronger collector appeal from the Nightmare Frame treatment and horror aesthetic. Neither set reaches the EV heights of Modern Horizons 3 from the same year but both offer reasonable value for the price point.
