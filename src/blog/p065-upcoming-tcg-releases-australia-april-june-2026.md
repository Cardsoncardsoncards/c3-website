### MTG Secrets of Strixhaven — 24 April 2026

The main Magic: The Gathering set for spring 2026. Returns to Strixhaven University on the Arcavios plane, a proper MTG-native setting rather than a Universes Beyond crossover. Features the Mystical Archive bonus sheet returning with Force of Will reprint, a serialised headliner card (Emeritus of Ideation, 500 copies globally), five Commander precon decks, and new mechanics including Prepare and Opus.

**Prerelease:** 17 to 23 April 2026 at local game stores.

**Products available on Amazon AU:** Play Booster Display (30 packs), Codex Bundle, Silverquill Influence Commander Deck, Witherbloom Pestilence Commander Deck. Pre-orders live now.

**Key chase cards:** Emeritus of Ideation (serialised, 500 globally), Ral Zarek borderless planeswalker, five borderless Elder Dragons, Force of Will Mystical Archive reprint, Japanese Mystical Archive variants.
