## What Is Outlaws of Thunder Junction and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Outlaws of Thunder Junction is a standard-legal Play Booster set with 36 packs per box. The Breaking News bonus sheet contains 30 reprints of historically powerful spells — instants, sorceries, and enchantments — in a special wanted poster card frame. Every pack contains one Breaking News card alongside the regular set cards. The bonus sheet includes format staples like Collected Company, Rite of Flame, Thoughtseize, and Tainted Pact in the special treatment. Foil and special treatment Breaking News cards command the highest premiums.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the Breaking News bonus sheet?**
Breaking News is a 30-card bonus set of reprints printed in a wanted poster card frame that appears in every Outlaws of Thunder Junction pack. Each pack contains one Breaking News card. The bonus sheet includes powerful spells from across MTG history including Collected Company, Rite of Flame, Thoughtseize, and Tainted Pact. These cards can be worth AU$2-30+ in their Breaking News treatment.

**Is Outlaws of Thunder Junction worth opening in Australia?**
OTJ has above-average EV for a 2024 standard set specifically because of the Breaking News bonus sheet. A high-value Breaking News card in foil or special treatment can significantly boost your box result. At AU$180 for 36 packs it compares favourably to other 2024 standard releases. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current assessment.

**What villain planeswalkers are in Outlaws of Thunder Junction?**
Oko the Ringleader, Vraska the Silencer, Kellan the Daring, and other returning villain characters appear as planeswalkers and legendary creatures. Oko in particular has a history of being banned in multiple formats and generates collector interest alongside competitive demand.

**How does OTJ compare to Wilds of Eldraine for EV?**
Both sets benefit from a bonus sheet mechanism. Wilds of Eldraine's Enchanting Tales bonus sheet is arguably stronger for Commander players due to the density of Commander staples. OTJ's Breaking News sheet is more competitive-format focused. Both sets sit above the standard set average for EV.
