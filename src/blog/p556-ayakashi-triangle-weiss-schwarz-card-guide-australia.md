# Ayakashi Triangle in Weiss Schwarz: Matsuri, Suzu and Omiko City

Ayakashi Triangle brings Matsuri Kazamaki and Suzu Kanade into Weiss Schwarz through a confirmed same-day trial deck and Booster Pack launch, built around the 《Omiko City》 character trait and a genuine Matsuri and Suzu partnership mechanic. If you already play and want the partnership detail, jump ahead. If you are newer to Weiss Schwarz, the primer below covers the core rules, with the full mechanics glossary linked at the end.

## The set at a glance

Ayakashi Triangle is a single stand-alone set, released 16 August 2024, with a total of 211 cards. It has not received a second release since, so this remains the complete Ayakashi Triangle collection currently available.

## Quick primer: how Weiss Schwarz works

Every deck is 50 cards split across colours, cards get stronger as your clock (a face-down pile built from damage taken) grows, climax cards (up to 8 per deck) combo with character abilities on the right turn, and you can run up to four copies of any named card. For the full breakdown, read our [complete rules glossary](/blog/how-weiss-schwarz-works-rules-deck-building-australia).
