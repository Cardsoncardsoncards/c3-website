Mono-blue Commander is associated with two things: drawing cards and countering spells. Both are true. Blue has the deepest card-draw in the format and the strongest counter suite. What it lacks in creatures, it makes up for in consistency and control.

This guide covers the best mono-blue Commanders available in Australia with current AUD pricing.

## What Mono-Blue Does Well

Blue is the undisputed king of card advantage. Spells like Rhystic Study, Mystic Remora, and Windfall generate more cards than almost anything in other colours. Blue also has the best tutors for artifacts (Fabricate, Whir of Invention) and the strongest counterspell suite.

Mono-blue Commander strategies tend toward one of three directions: artifact-based combo, big-mana spells, or hard control.

## Top Mono-Blue Commanders

### Urza, Lord High Artificer

The gold standard for mono-blue artifact commanders. Urza makes your artifacts produce mana, creates Construct tokens, and allows you to cast spells by tapping any artifact. In a deck full of artifacts, Urza provides enormous mana advantages and wins through card advantage or combo.

**Why it works**: generates mana, generates tokens, enables explosive turns.
**Current AU price**: approximately AU$15 to AU$35.

### Talrand, Sky Summoner

Every instant or sorcery you cast creates a 2/2 Drake token. Talrand rewards a spell-heavy build and fills the board while maintaining card advantage.

**Why it works**: passive token generation, scales with the number of spells you run, clear game plan.
**Current AU price**: approximately AU$2 to AU$5. One of the best budget options in mono-blue.

### Baral, Chief of Compliance

All your spells cost one less mana, and whenever a spell is countered, you loot (draw and discard). Baral rewards playing control and synergises with the myriad ways blue counters and filters.

**Why it works**: cost reduction makes it possible to cast multiple spells per turn, loot trigger generates card advantage.
**Current AU price**: approximately AU$3 to AU$8.

### Azami, Lady of Scrolls

Azami taps to draw a card if you have a Wizard in play. In a deck full of Wizards, this generates massive card advantage every turn.

**Why it works**: tribal synergy, consistent card draw, enables combo finishes with Mind Over Matter.
**Current AU price**: approximately AU$4 to AU$10.

### Jin-Gitaxias, Core Augur

An 8-mana bomb that draws 7 cards at your end step, forces opponents to discard down to 0 at theirs, and sets a 5-power flash blocker in play. One of the most powerful mono-blue commanders when resolved.

**Why it works**: immediately takes over the game, flash keyword allows surprise deployment.
**Current AU price**: varies by printing from AU$5 to AU$20+.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best Commander deck for a new player in Australia?
Any Commander preconstructed deck from a recent set is a good starting point. Pick the theme or colour combination that appeals to you most. Current options from Tarkir: Dragonstorm and Lorwyn Eclipsed are available on Amazon AU.

### Can I use a Commander precon in tournament play?
Commander preconstructed decks are legal for casual Commander play and official Commander events. The individual cards are legal in Commander, Legacy, and Vintage. The precon as a whole is not competitive at high-level play but works fine for regular Commander nights.
