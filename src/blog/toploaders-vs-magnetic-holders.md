## Top Loaders vs Magnetic Cases: The Right Tool for the Right Card

If you are storing or shipping valuable TCG cards in Australia, you need to understand the difference between a top loader and a magnetic one-touch case. Using the wrong one costs money — either through card damage or overkill on protection you did not need.

## Top Loaders

A top loader is a rigid PVC sleeve open at the top. Cards slide in from the top and are held by friction and the sleeve beneath them. They are the standard for protecting individual cards during shipping and storage.

**When to use them:** Any card worth AU$5 to $100. Singles orders shipped through eBay. Bulk storage of valuable cards.

**Key sizes:**
- **35pt:** Standard card thickness. MTG, Pokemon, Lorcana, One Piece all use 35pt.
- **55pt:** Thick cards — ETB promo cards, oversized cards, some Collector Booster exclusives.
- **100pt and above:** For very thick cards like sketch cards, extended art versions on card stock.

## Magnetic One-Touch Cases

A magnetic case (commonly called a one-touch) uses two rigid acrylic panels held together by magnets. The card sits in a recessed slot with no friction — the magnets hold it shut. UV protection is standard on most quality one-touches.

**When to use them:** Cards worth AU$100 and above. Grading submissions. Display pieces. Anything you want to show off without removing from the case.

**Point rating matters:** A 35pt one-touch fits a standard card. A 55pt or 75pt one-touch is for thick cards or cards in a penny sleeve. Always measure your card thickness before buying.

## The Right Call by Card Value

| Card Value | Protection |
|---|---|
| Under AU$5 | Penny sleeve only |
| AU$5 to $50 | Penny sleeve + top loader |
| AU$50 to $200 | Penny sleeve + top loader + team bag |
| AU$200+ | Penny sleeve + one-touch |
| Grading submission | Penny sleeve + semi-rigid + top loader |

## Shipping on eBay

If you sell on eBay AU, the standard is penny sleeve plus 35pt top loader plus team bag for cards up to AU$100. For cards above AU$100, replace the top loader with a one-touch and add bubble wrap around it before placing in the envelope.
