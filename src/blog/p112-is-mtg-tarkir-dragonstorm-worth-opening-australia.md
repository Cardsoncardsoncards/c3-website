**What are the five dragon khan mythics?**
Each of the five Tarkir clans has a legendary dragon warlord as a mythic rare — one for each of the five three-colour combinations (Abzan, Jeskai, Sultai, Mardu, and Temur). These are the headline collector targets from the set. Borderless full-art versions of these five cards command the highest premiums in the AU secondary market.

**How does Tarkir: Dragonstorm compare to the original Khans of Tarkir?**
The original Khans of Tarkir (2014) is widely regarded as one of the best draft sets in MTG history and produced powerful cards like Siege Rhino and Treasure Cruise. Dragonstorm is a different product designed for the Play Booster era. The return generates nostalgia demand but the two sets are separate products with different card pools and EV profiles.

**Does Tarkir: Dragonstorm have Commander support?**
Yes. The dragon tribal theme provides direct Commander support for dragon-focused decks across multiple archetypes. The five clan commanders work in five different three-colour combinations meaning most Commander players can find a relevant dragon khan for their existing deck colours.
