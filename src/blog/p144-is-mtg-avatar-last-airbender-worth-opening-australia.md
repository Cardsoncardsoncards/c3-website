## What Is Avatar: The Last Airbender and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

MTG Avatar: The Last Airbender is a Universes Beyond crossover using the Play Booster format with 30 packs per box. The four nations — Air Nomads, Water Tribe, Earth Kingdom, and Fire Nation — are represented across the colour pairs. Aang, Zuko, Katara, Toph, Sokka, Azula, and Uncle Iroh appear as legendary creatures with abilities reflecting their elemental bending and character roles. Special treatments include borderless versions of major characters and serialised versions of the central cast. The four elemental bending abilities inform the mechanical design of key cards.

Katara and Toph in their elemental mastery forms are Commander targets worth AU$10-25+. Uncle Iroh as a legendary creature driving wisdom-themed Commander strategies has strong appeal. Any serialised version of a main cast member is an exceptional collector pull.
