## What Is Modern Horizons 2 and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Modern Horizons 2 was designed entirely for non-rotating formats — Modern, Legacy, and Commander — with zero Standard legality. Every card had to be powerful enough to justify inclusion in those formats, meaning the floor value of every rare is significantly higher than any standard set. The Set Booster box contains 30 packs. The Collector Booster box contains 12 packs with higher foil frequency and retro frame treatments including original dual land reprints in foil that can be worth AU$200-500+ each.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why is Ragavan Nimble Pilferer so valuable?**
Ragavan is a one-mana legendary creature that deals damage equal to its power and creates a Treasure token whenever it deals combat damage to a player. It also exiles the top card of the opponent's library and lets you cast it. A one-mana 2/1 with Dash that generates value every turn it connects is considered by many players to be the most powerful one-drop ever printed in MTG. It is a four-of in most Modern aggressive decks.

**What are the Evoke Elementals and why are they Modern staples?**
The five Evoke Elementals (Solitude, Endurance, Grief, Subtlety, Fury) are mythic creatures with Evoke costs that let you cast them for free by exiling a card of a matching colour. Evoking them causes the enter and leave-the-battlefield triggers to both fire simultaneously, generating a one-time effect for zero mana. This efficiency at zero mana cost made all five immediate format staples. Fury has since been banned in Modern but the others remain legal and heavily played.

**What are retro frame dual land reprints?**
Modern Horizons 2 contains reprints of original dual lands (Volcanic Island, Bayou, etc.) and other powerful lands in the retro old-border card frame from pre-8th Edition MTG. These retro frame versions are exclusive to Collector Boosters in foil. Non-foil retro frame versions appear in Set Boosters. Any retro frame dual land is an exceptional pull worth AU$60-400+ depending on the land and treatment.

**Is MH2 the best MTG product for EV in Australia?**
Modern Horizons 2 consistently ranks as one of the top two or three MTG products for EV alongside Ultimate Masters and Double Masters 2022. The combination of Ragavan, the Evoke Elementals, and retro frame dual lands creates a set where even a modest box result covers a significant portion of the AU$180 purchase price.
