# Arifureta in Weiss Schwarz: Hajime's Harem Across Four Colours

Arifureta brings Hajime Nagumo and his companions into Weiss Schwarz through the confirmed "From Commonplace to World's Strongest" release, with card listings documenting a genuinely broad colour spread across yellow, red, green, and blue for this single set. If you already play and want the character and colour breakdown, jump ahead. If you are newer to Weiss Schwarz, the primer below covers the core rules, with the full mechanics glossary linked at the end.

## The set at a glance

Arifureta is a single stand-alone set, released 17 November 2023, with a total of 192 cards. It has not received a second release since, so this remains the complete Arifureta collection currently available.

## Quick primer: how Weiss Schwarz works

Every deck is 50 cards split across colours, cards get stronger as your clock (a face-down pile built from damage taken) grows, climax cards (up to 8 per deck) combo with character abilities on the right turn, and you can run up to four copies of any named card. For the full breakdown, read our [complete rules glossary](/blog/how-weiss-schwarz-works-rules-deck-building-australia).

## A Genuinely Wide Colour Spread Around One Central Cast

Arifureta's confirmed core cast spans Hajime Nagumo, Yue, Shea Haulia, Tio Klarus, Kaori Shirasaki, Shizuku Yaegashi, Myu, and Aiko Hatayama. What stands out is how many colours this cast spans, community card listings document cards across yellow, red, green, and blue within this single set, a wider spread than many single-set properties settle into. Rather than each companion being locked to one shared colour identity, the cast's individual card lines pull from different parts of the colour wheel, giving deck builders a genuinely wide starting palette to choose from even within one confirmed release. The practical first decision is not just which companion to build around, but which colour pairing that choice commits you to, since Hajime plus Yue draws from a different colour combination than Hajime plus Shea or Hajime plus Tio would, closer in spirit to how Attack on Titan spreads its four colours across different story factions.
