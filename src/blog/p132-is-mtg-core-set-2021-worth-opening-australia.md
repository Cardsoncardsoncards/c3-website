## What Is Core Set 2021 and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Core Set 2021 is structured around the planeswalker Teferi and his history across the MTG storyline. The set includes five planeswalker-centred Showcase card treatments presenting versions of key planeswalkers in stained glass art styles. The Draft Booster box contains 36 packs. The stained glass Showcase treatment has been popular with collectors for its distinctive art style. Core sets are designed to be approachable for new players while still containing powerful enough cards for experienced players.

## Chase Cards and What to Look For

Ugin the Spirit Dragon is the headline mythic — a colourless planeswalker that exiles all nonland, non-colourless permanents with his ultimate ability and deals damage through his minus ability. Colourless casting cost makes him a Commander staple in virtually every deck. Worth AU$12-25+. Teferi Master of Time is a unique four-mana planeswalker that can activate on every player's turn — both yours and opponents'. Worth AU$10-20+. Mangara the Diplomat is one of the most played white Commander cards drawing cards when opponents attack or cast multiple spells. Azusa Lost but Seeking is a Legacy and Commander staple worth AU$8-18+.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Core Set 2021 worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$140 for 36 packs, Core Set 2021 has one of the best per-pack cost bases of any MTG product on Amazon AU. Ugin, Teferi, and Mangara as Commander staples anchor the mythic value. The stained glass Showcase treatment attracts collector demand. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current personalised assessment.

**What makes Ugin the Spirit Dragon a universal Commander staple?**
Ugin is a colourless planeswalker — his casting cost contains no coloured mana symbols. This means he can be played in any Commander deck regardless of colour identity. His minus ability deals damage to target creature or planeswalker for X colourless mana. His ultimate exiles all permanents that are not colourless or lands. The combination of accessibility across all deck types and a powerful board-wiping ultimate makes him universally useful.

**What is unique about Teferi Master of Time?**
Teferi Master of Time can activate his planeswalker abilities on any player's turn — not just yours. This means he can activate twice per round table rotation in a four-player Commander game. His plus ability phases out a target permanent or player. His minus draws cards and puts one back. The ability to activate on opponents' turns creates unique defensive and political plays.

**Is Core Set 2021 a good product for new MTG players?**
Yes. Core sets are designed to be accessible entry points with cleaner mechanics and simpler card text than the most recent standard set. The lower AU$140 price point and the broad Commander appeal of key mythics make it a reasonable starting purchase for players new to booster products who also want cards relevant to ongoing play.
