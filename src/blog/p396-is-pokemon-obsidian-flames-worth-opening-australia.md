## What Is in Obsidian Flames?

Obsidian Flames introduced Tera-type Pokemon ex as a new visual and mechanical variant. Tera Pokemon feature crystal-type backgrounds and effects, and the Tera Charizard ex became the defining card of this era. The set has 230 standard cards plus Secret Rare treatments.

**Set size:** Standard SV-era structure with regular cards, Illustration Rares, Special Illustration Rares, and Hyper Rares.

**Packs per box:** 36 packs per standard booster box, 10 cards per pack.

**Pull structure:** Approximately one Ultra Rare per two packs on average. Special Illustration Rares appear roughly once per case of six boxes under standard pull rates, though set-specific guarantees vary.

## Top Cards to Know

**Charizard ex Special Illustration Rare (Tera)** is the headline card and defines this set's collector value in Australia. **Tyranitar ex Special Illustration Rare** and **Dragonite ex SIR** are strong secondary chase targets. Competitive players target Pidgeot ex, which became a format staple as a consistency engine.
