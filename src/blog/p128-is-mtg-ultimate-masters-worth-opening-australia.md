## What Is Ultimate Masters and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Ultimate Masters contains 254 reprints targeting Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander formats. The mythic and rare lists include Snapcaster Mage, Liliana of the Veil, Dark Depths, Cavern of Souls, Emrakul the Aeons Torn, Goryo's Vengeance, Through the Breach, and dozens of other high-value staples. Every box includes one Box Topper card — an oversized (but not playable in this format) premium alternate art version of a key set card. The Draft Booster box contains 24 packs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the Box Topper and is it included with Amazon AU purchases?**
The Box Topper is one oversized premium alternate art card included with each Ultimate Masters booster box. It is a physically larger card with alternate art — not playable in sanctioned tournament play due to size but fully collectible. Box Toppers range from AU$30 (lower-demand cards) to AU$200+ (Snapcaster Mage, Liliana of the Veil). Amazon AU booster boxes should include the Box Topper, but confirm with the listing before purchasing.

**Is Ultimate Masters the best MTG reprint set ever?**
Ultimate Masters consistently ranks alongside Double Masters 2022 and Modern Horizons 2 as the top three MTG reprint products for EV. UMA has the advantage of the Box Topper guarantee and a reprint list where every rare is Legacy or Vintage relevant. MH2 has a higher average individual card value. DM22 has the double rare guarantee. All three are exceptional products — which is best depends on your format preferences.

**Has Snapcaster Mage been reprinted since Ultimate Masters?**
Snapcaster Mage was reprinted in Time Spiral Remastered (2021) in the retro frame Timeshifted slot. Prior to UMA it had only appeared in Innistrad (2011) and a Grand Prix promo version. The multiple printings have reduced the price from AU$100+ to AU$25-60+, but it remains one of the most played cards in Legacy and Modern.

**Why is Dark Depths valuable?**
Dark Depths is a Legendary Land that enters with ten Ice counters and has an activated ability to remove one counter. When the last counter is removed, you sacrifice Dark Depths and create a 20/20 flying indestructible token. In Legacy and Modern, this is accelerated through Thespian's Stage (which can copy Dark Depths and immediately has no counters) creating the 20/20 token for just two mana. The Dark Depths plus Thespian's Stage combo is the foundation of multiple Legacy decks.
