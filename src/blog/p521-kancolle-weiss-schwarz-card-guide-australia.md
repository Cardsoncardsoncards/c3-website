# Kancolle in Weiss Schwarz: Warships, Colours and a Compact Catalogue

Kancolle brings one of the more distinctive premises in Weiss Schwarz to the table, its cast is built from personified warships drawn from WWII-era naval history, reimagined as characters. It is a smaller but genuinely unique catalogue compared to the sprawling multi-arc franchises elsewhere in the game. If you already play and want the specifics, jump ahead. ## The set at a glance

Kancolle spans 4 tracked releases, from the original KanColle set (15 May 2015) through Arrival! Reinforcement Fleets from Europe! in November 2018, totalling 670 cards. It has not received a new release since, so this remains the complete Kancolle collection currently tracked.

## Quick primer: how Weiss Schwarz works

Every deck is 50 cards split across colours, cards get stronger as your clock (a face-down pile built from damage taken) grows, climax cards (up to 8 per deck) combo with character abilities on the right turn, and you can run up to four copies of any named card.
