## Why Vendetta Matters for Australian Collectors

The fourth set in any TCG represents a meaningful point in the game's maturity. The card pool is large enough for genuine deckbuilding diversity, the competitive scene is established, and the collector base is growing. Vendetta's position as the first set after the game's initial three-set launch window makes it particularly significant for players who started with Origins and have built through Unleashed. New champions in Vendetta will create new demand for cards across all four sets, potentially shifting prices on older singles.

## Pre-Ordering Vendetta in Australia

Australian pre-orders for Riftbound Vendetta are available through local game stores and online Australian TCG retailers. Pre-order pricing for booster boxes typically matches the retail price of the current set. For Unleashed comparison, boxes have been available for AU$299.99 at Vault Games. Vendetta pricing will be confirmed by distributors closer to launch. Pre-ordering locks in availability at launch pricing before any secondary market premium applies if the set is popular.

## Should You Pre-Order Vendetta Sealed?

Pre-ordering sealed product for any new set carries the same risk as opening any box: you do not know what the top cards are worth until the set releases and the secondary market settles. The safest approach for Australian buyers is to wait for the first two weeks of sales data after Vendetta releases, check the [EV Calculator at /tools](/tools) with current Vendetta card prices, and then decide whether box opening or targeted singles buying delivers better value at that specific price point. Hype pricing on pre-release booster boxes rarely reflects post-launch equilibrium pricing.

## Vendetta in the Competitive Context

New sets shift the competitive Riftbound meta. Champions introduced in Vendetta will create new archetypes, and some existing Unleashed champions may become stronger through new support cards while others may be weakened by new hate cards. Australian players who are already competitive should watch the early Vendetta previews and community discussion to understand which champions are being strengthened and which new archetypes are arriving. That intelligence is more valuable than opening boxes at launch hoping to pull the right cards.
