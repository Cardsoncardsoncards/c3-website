## Why Mega Evolution Pitch Black Matters

Mega Evolution has been absent from the Standard competitive format since the transition from the XY era to the Sun and Moon era in the mid-2010s. Its return under the SV framework is a genuine event for the Pokemon TCG community, not just a marketing exercise. Mega Evolution Pokemon have historically been among the most powerful and most collectible cards in the game's history, with original mega cards like Mega Charizard EX, Mega Blastoise EX, and Mega Mewtwo EX still holding collector value today.

Pitch Black appears to be the first of at least two Mega Evolution sets in 2026, with the 30th Celebration set following in September. The two-set approach means the Mega Evolution era is being given room to breathe in the meta rather than being a single-set gimmick.

For collectors, the return of Mega Evolution means new chase cards featuring iconic Mega Pokemon. Expect the usual assortment of full arts, special illustration rares, and hyper rares featuring Mega forms of popular Pokemon. These will be the high-value pulls from Pitch Black booster boxes.
