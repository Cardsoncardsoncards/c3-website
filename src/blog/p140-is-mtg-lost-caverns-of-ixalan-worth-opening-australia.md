## What Is The Lost Caverns of Ixalan and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

**What is the Discover mechanic?**
Discover X is similar to Cascade — when you cast a spell with Discover, you reveal cards from the top of your library until you find a card with mana value X or less, then you may cast it for free. Unlike Cascade, you choose the value X, giving more control over what you find. The mechanic created powerful combo interactions in Pioneer and Commander driving competitive demand for Discover cards.

**What is the Cosmium foil treatment?**
Cosmium foil is a special foiling treatment used in Lost Caverns of Ixalan applying a distinctive golden iridescent finish to special versions of cards. The treatment reflects light with a warm gold-tinged colour shift, referencing the ancient civilisation's golden treasures discovered in the caverns. Cosmium foil versions of popular mythics command premiums of AU$5-20+ above regular card prices.

**How does Lost Caverns of Ixalan compare to the original Ixalan for tribal Commander?**
The original Ixalan (2017) established the four tribes but is not on Amazon AU at accessible prices. Lost Caverns of Ixalan builds on that tribal foundation with new commanders and payoffs that are generally more powerful than the 2017 originals. For tribal Commander players, Lost Caverns is the more relevant set for current deck building.
