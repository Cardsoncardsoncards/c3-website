## What Is Iconic Masters and Why Does It Matter for Australian Players?

Iconic Masters contains 249 reprints targeting Modern, Legacy, Vintage, and Commander formats. The mythic list includes Mana Drain, Ancestral Vision, Consecrated Sphinx, Archangel of Thune, and Aetherling. The rare list features Aether Vial, Grove of the Burnwillows, Horizon Canopy (original printing), and other format staples. The Draft Booster box contains 24 packs. No Collector Booster exists for this set — all premium versions are found within the draft booster.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Iconic Masters worth opening in Australia?**
At AU$160 for 24 packs, Iconic Masters offers moderate and predictable EV with Mana Drain as the headline target. The fully settled secondary market means lower variance than newer releases. Use the C3 EV Calculator for a current personalised assessment.

**What is Mana Drain and why is it valuable?**
Mana Drain is a two-mana instant that counters a target spell and adds colourless mana equal to that spell's mana cost to your mana pool at the beginning of your next main phase. This means countering a five-mana spell effectively gives you five free mana next turn. It is restricted in Vintage (only one copy allowed) due to its power. Worth AU$30-80+ in the AU secondary market.

**How does Iconic Masters compare to Ultimate Masters for EV?**
Ultimate Masters significantly outperforms Iconic Masters in EV terms. UMA contains Snapcaster Mage, Liliana of the Veil, Dark Depths, and the Box Topper guarantee — all higher-value than Iconic Masters equivalents. Iconic Masters at AU$160 is more accessible but offers substantially lower average EV. If choosing between them at similar price points, Ultimate Masters is the superior product.

**Are there any Legacy combo pieces in Iconic Masters?**
Yes. Ancestral Vision enables Vision Cascade combo decks in Modern and Legacy. Through the Breach (also in the set) is a Legacy and Modern combo piece for Emrakul decks. Grove of the Burnwillows enables Punishing Fire lock in Legacy. The set has several Legacy-relevant cards beyond just Mana Drain.
